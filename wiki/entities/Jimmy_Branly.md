# Jimmy Branly

Jimmy Branly is a master percussionist and author specializing in the application of traditional Afro-Cuban rhythms to the modern drum set.

### Contributions to Curriculum
His book, *The New Method for Afro-Cuban Drumming*, provides the basis for the [[Montuno_Track.md]] unit. His teaching focuses on:
- **Clave Integration**: Understanding how the 2-3 and 3-2 clave patterns influence drum set orchestration.
- **Modern Mambo**: Adapting traditional percussion parts (Timbales, Congas, Bongos) to the kit.
