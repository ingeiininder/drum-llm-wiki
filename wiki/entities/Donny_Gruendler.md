# Donny Gruendler
Donny Gruendler is a highly accomplished drummer, educator, author, and producer, known for his diverse musical background and significant contributions to percussion education.

## Background and Influences
Born and raised in Detroit, Michigan, Gruendler was exposed to a wide array of musical genres from an early age, including [[Soul]], [[Funk]], [[Pop]], [[Hip Hop]], [[Traditional Swing]], [[Hard Bop]], [[Techno]], [[House]], and [[Blues]]. This diverse upbringing shaped his versatile musical approach.

## Education
*   **Berklee College of Music**: Graduated at age 20 with a Bachelor of Music degree.
*   **Wayne State University**: Earned a Master of Music degree at age 21.

## Performance and Recording Career
Now based in Los Angeles, Gruendler has an extensive performance, programming, touring, and recording resume. He has worked with notable artists such as:
*   Kenny Burrell
*   John Medeski
*   DJ Logic
*   The Funk Brothers
*   DJ A-Ski (Unique 74)
*   Rick Holmstrom
*   Kirk Fletcher

He has also composed and/or played on jingles for brands like Axe Body Spray, RE/MAX on the Boulevard, and NPR. His film and TV credits include "Last Holiday," "Father of Invention," and "Chicago Overcoat."

## Production Work
Under the pseudonym "Inc," Donny Gruendler is also a noted producer. He is one half of the funky-soul-electro duo Rhett Frazier Inc., whose productions have been described by Okayplayer as "cosmic brilliance delivered via a well-stirred pot of soul, jazz, rock, funk and gospel."

## Educational Contributions
Gruendler holds a prominent position in music education:
*   **Musicians Institute (MI)**: Vice President of Instruction and Curricular Development in Hollywood, California.
*   **Modern Drummer**: Member of the education team and a frequent columnist for the magazine.
*   **Author**: He has released several instructional books, DVDs, and other online/digital content under his own name, including co-authoring the [[Drum Reading 1]] curriculum with [[Stewart Jean]].

For more information, his website is www.donnygruendler.com.
