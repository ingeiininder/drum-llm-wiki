# Charley_Wilcoxon_Solo_No_5

**Solo No. 5** from the Charley Wilcoxon collection is a 16-bar rudimental study in **2/4 time**. While previous solos may have focused on flams and paradiddles, Solo No. 5 is primarily a study in **Drag applications**.

### Rudimental Breakdown
*   **Drags**: The primary focus. Includes Single Drags and their integration into rhythmic phrases.
*   **Lesson 25**: Featured prominently in Bars 11, 12, and 15.
*   **Flams**: Used in conjunction with drags and paradiddles (e.g., Flam Paradiddles in Bar 4).
*   **Alternating Flams**: Utilized as eighth notes in Bar 3.

### Performance Notes
1.  **Mechanics First**: Master the rhythms and stickings at a slow tempo before adding dynamics.
2.  **Dynamic Life**: Once the mechanics are fluid, apply the written dynamic markings to transform the exercise into a musical performance.
3.  **Drag Quality**: Ensure the grace notes of the [[Drag_Rudiment]] are tight and consistent, leading into the primary stroke.
