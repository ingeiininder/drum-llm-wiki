# MI Drum Reading 2 by Donny Gruendler and Stewart Jean

This is the second installment in the [[Musicians_Institute]]'s [[Drum_Reading]] series, co-authored by renowned educators [[Donny_Gruendler]] and [[Stewart_Jean]]. It is a recommended [[Method Book]] for drummers seeking to advance their [[Rhythm_Reading]] skills beyond foundational concepts.

**Purpose:**
*   To build upon the rhythmic vocabulary introduced in MI Drum Reading 1.
*   To introduce more complex rhythmic figures, [[Time_Signature]]s, and musical concepts.
*   To provide structured exercises for daily practice, essential for honing [[Sight_Reading]] abilities.

**Pro-Tip:**
When working through this book, focus not just on playing the notes correctly, but on understanding the underlying rhythmic patterns and how they relate to musical phrases. This will help you apply these reading skills to actual musical situations.
