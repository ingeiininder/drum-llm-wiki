# Motown
**Motown** is a legendary R&B record label founded by **Berry Gordy** in 1959 in Detroit, Michigan (the "Motor Town"). It became a definitive sub-genre of R&B, blending soulful melodies with pop sensibilities.

### The Funk Brothers
The "Motown Sound" was defined by a core group of studio musicians known as **The Funk Brothers**. Their task was to create drum parts that were both song-oriented and danceable.

### Key Motown Drummers
*   **[[Benny Benjamin]]**
*   **[[Richard "Pistol" Allen]]**
*   **[[Uriel Jones]]**

### Stylistic Legacy
The Motown sound continues to influence modern artists such as Adele, Bruno Mars, and Amy Winehouse.
