# Dal Segno (D.S.)
[[Dal Segno (D.S.)]] is a musical term meaning "from the sign." In [[Notation]], it instructs the musician to return to a specific point in the music marked by the "Segno" symbol (𝄋).

This symbol is a crucial component of navigation instructions like [[D.S._al_Coda]], where it indicates the starting point for a repeat section before proceeding to a [[Coda]] or other ending.

**The Segno Symbol**: 𝄋

**Pro-Tip**: When reading a chart, always locate the [[Dal Segno (D.S.)]] symbol early on if you see a "D.S." instruction. Knowing its position is vital for accurately navigating the [[Song Structure]] and avoiding mistakes during performance. It's common for the Segno to be placed at the beginning of a verse or chorus that is meant to be repeated.
