# Stax
**Stax Records** was a prominent R&B label founded in 1957 by Jim Stewart and Estelle Axton in Memphis, Tennessee. While Motown was often associated with a "pop-R&B" polish, Stax was known for a grittier, southern soul sound.

### Booker T. and the MG's
Similar to Motown's Funk Brothers, Stax utilized a house band to back their artist roster. This band, **Booker T. and the MG's**, featured the highly influential drummer **[[Al Jackson Jr.]]**.

### Musical Philosophy
The drummers at Stax focused on "composing" drum parts that perfectly fit the song's emotional core while maintaining a danceable pulse.
