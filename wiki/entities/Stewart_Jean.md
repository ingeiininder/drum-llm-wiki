# Stewart Jean

Stewart Jean is a respected drummer and educator, recognized for his "direct and to-the-point" drumming style and his dedication to music education. He currently serves as the **Program Chair for the Drum Program** at the [[Musicians_Institute]] (MI) in Hollywood, California.

## Background and Career
- **Roots**: Raised in New Jersey, influenced by Classical music and Led Zeppelin.
- **Education**: Graduate of the Studio Music and Jazz Program at the **University of Miami**.
- **Performance**: A prolific freelance musician who has worked with artists including **Jimmy Buffett**, **Raul Midon**, and **Preston Smith**.

## Educational Contributions
- **Curriculum Development**: Developed diverse curricula for MI, including:
    - [[Analysis_of_Great_Grooves]]
    - [[Drum_Set_Soloing]]
    - [[Introduction_to_Keyboards]]
- **Author**: Co-author of [[Drum_Reading_1]] and [[Drum_Performance_1]] with [[Donny_Gruendler]].
