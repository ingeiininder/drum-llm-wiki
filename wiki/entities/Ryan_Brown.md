# Ryan Brown

**Ryan Brown** is a professional drummer, percussionist, and educator based in Los Angeles. He is a prominent member of the drum faculty at [[Musicians Institute]] and contributed to the development of the school's percussion curriculum.

### Career Highlights
*   **Education**: Studied under rock legend **Kenny Aronoff** at Indiana University, earning a Bachelor of Music in Jazz Studies and Percussion.
*   **Performance**: Best known as the drummer for **Zappa Plays Zappa**. He has also performed with artists such as Ingrid Michaelson, Frank Stallone, and the Norwegian Wind Ensemble.
*   **Session Work**: Credits include the hit single "The Real Thing" by Bo Bice, and soundtracks for major films including *Kung Fu Panda*, *The Dark Knight*, and *Ice Age: Continental Drift*.
*   **Collaborations**: Has recorded with Alex Lifeson, Earl Slick, and Rudy Sarzo.

Brown's expertise spans rock, jazz, and orchestral percussion, making him a versatile contributor to the MI curriculum series.
