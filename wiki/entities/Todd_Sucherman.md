# Todd Sucherman

**Todd Sucherman** is a world-renowned drummer, best known for his long-standing tenure with the rock band **Styx**. He is recognized in the Musicians Institute curriculum for his technical precision, mastery of [[Odd-Time_Signatures]], and sophisticated [[Linear_Drumming]] concepts.

### Contributions to Curriculum
Sucherman's work on the track [[Raven]] (from his book *Methods & Mechanics*) is used to teach students:
- Advanced [[Coordination]] through displaced [[Hi-Hat_Ostinato]] patterns.
- Creative use of drum textures (e.g., playing with **snares off** and utilizing **tom rims**).
- Phrasing across bar lines in mixed meters (6/8 and 9/8).

### Style and Influence
Sucherman's style blends the power of arena rock with the finesse of jazz and fusion. His educational materials focus heavily on the "mechanics" of drumming—ensuring that physical movement is optimized for maximum sonic output and musicality.
