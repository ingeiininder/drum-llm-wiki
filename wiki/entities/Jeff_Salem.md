# Jeff Salem

**Jeff Salem** is a prominent percussionist and educator, contributing several key performance pieces to the Musicians Institute *Project Recording 2* curriculum. His compositions are primarily used to teach [[Funk]] sensibilities and [[Locking_with_the_Bass]].

### Key Works in Curriculum
- **[[Saturday_Night_in_Memphis]]**: Focuses on [[Comping_Horn_Figures]] and ensemble interaction.
- **[[Burn_the_Bridge]]**: Focuses on [[Syncopation]] and navigating [[Odd_Phrasing]] (7-bar patterns).
- **Stab**: (Unit 8) Further explores aggressive rhythmic punctuations.

### Educational Philosophy
Salem’s tracks emphasize "playing for the song." His exercises often strip away unnecessary complexity to focus on the "pocket"—the fundamental alignment between the drummer and the bass player.
