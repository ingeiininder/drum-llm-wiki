# Thomas Lang

Thomas Lang is a world-renowned session drummer and educator known for his extreme technical proficiency and "Creative Control" system.

### Contributions to Curriculum
Lang's work is featured in the MI curriculum through tracks like [[Angel_Track.md]] and "Dog." His approach emphasizes:
- **Multi-Pedal Orchestration**: Using the feet as melodic and rhythmic voices.
- **Technical Independence**: Developing the ability to play complex polyrhythms and linear patterns.
- **Applied Technique**: The use of [[Finger Control vs. Wrist Control]] to achieve high-speed textures in modern genres like [[Drum and Bass]].

**Key Works**:
- *Creative Control* (Book/DVD)
- *Creative Coordination*
