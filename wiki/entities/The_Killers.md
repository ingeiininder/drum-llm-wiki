# The Killers
`[[The_Killers]]` are an American rock band formed in Las Vegas in 2001. Known for their anthemic sound, they blend elements of `[[New Wave]]`, `[[Post-Punk Revival]]`, and `[[Alternative Rock]]`. While often associated with a more melodic and pop-oriented sound than some of their heavier contemporaries, their music frequently incorporates driving `[[16th_Notes]]` and `[[Syncopated_Beats]]` that contribute to the energetic `[[Rhythmic_Textures]]` characteristic of modern `[[Rock_Music_Styles]]`, as discussed in `[[Intro_to_Two-Handed_16ths]]`.
