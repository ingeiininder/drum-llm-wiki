# Al Jackson Jr.
**Al Jackson Jr.** was the primary drummer for the [[Stax]] record label and a founding member of **Booker T. and the MG's**. 

Known as "The Human Timekeeper," Jackson was celebrated for his impeccable feel, deep pocket, and ability to play exactly what the song required. His work defined the Memphis soul sound and influenced generations of R&B and rock drummers.
