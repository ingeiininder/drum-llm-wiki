# Nine Inch Nails
`[[Nine_Inch_Nails]]` is an American industrial rock band formed in 1988 by Trent Reznor. They are widely recognized for their pioneering role in industrial music and their significant influence on `[[Alternative Rock]]` and `[[Metal]]` genres. Their sound often incorporates aggressive electronic elements, `[[Syncopated_Beats]]`, and dark, atmospheric `[[Rhythmic_Textures]]`, embodying the fusion of `[[Synth-Pop]]` and `[[Heavy Metal]]` discussed in the context of `[[Intro_to_Two-Handed_16ths]]`. Their drumming often features intricate, driving patterns that contribute to their distinctive sound within `[[Rock_Music_Styles]]`.
