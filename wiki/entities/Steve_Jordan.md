# Steve Jordan
[[Steve_Jordan]] is a highly influential and respected American drummer, record producer, and composer. He is widely regarded as a "groove master" and is celebrated for his impeccable pocket, deep feel, and ability to blend various musical styles, particularly [[Funk]], [[Blues]], and [[Rock]]. He is notably the drummer for the [[John_Mayer_Trio]].

**Key Characteristics of His Drumming:**
*   **Deep Pocket:** Jordan is renowned for his incredibly solid and consistent rhythmic feel, often described as having a "deep pocket."
*   **Tasteful Syncopation:** He masterfully employs [[Syncopation]] to create intricate yet always musical drum patterns, often utilizing subtle [[Ghost_Notes]] and [[Rimshot]]s.
*   **Less is More:** His playing often exemplifies the "less is more" philosophy, focusing on groove, space, and musicality rather than excessive fills or technical displays.
*   **Versatility:** While deeply rooted in [[Funk]] and [[Blues]], he is highly versatile and has worked with a wide range of artists across many genres.

**Pro-Tip for Drummers:** To learn from [[Steve_Jordan]], don't just transcribe his beats; try to understand the *feel* behind them. Focus on your own [[Subdividing]] and internal clock. Practice playing with a [[Metronome]] to develop a rock-solid foundation, and then experiment with subtle variations and [[Syncopation]] to develop your own pocket. His work with the [[John_Mayer_Trio]] is an excellent starting point for study.
