# Coda
A [[Coda]] is a specific ending section of a musical piece. It provides a definitive conclusion to the composition, often differing from the main thematic material. In [[Notation]], the Coda is represented by a distinct symbol.

**The Coda Symbol**: 𝄌

When a musician encounters the Coda symbol in conjunction with instructions like [[D.S. al Coda]], it signifies that they should "jump" from their current position in the chart to the designated Coda section to conclude the song.

**Pro-Tip**: The Coda is not just an ending; it's an *intentional* ending. Drummers should approach the Coda with a clear understanding of its musical purpose. It might involve a change in [[Dynamics]], [[Tempo]], or [[Groove]] to create a sense of finality. Be prepared for any specific [[Drum Fills]] or hits that might be written into the Coda to punctuate the end of the song. Always locate the Coda section in the chart to ensure a smooth and accurate finish.
