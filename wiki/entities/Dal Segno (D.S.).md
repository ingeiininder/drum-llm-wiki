# Dal Segno (D.S.)
"Dal Segno" (often abbreviated as D.S.) is an Italian musical term meaning "from the sign." In musical notation, it refers to a specific symbol (a stylized 'S' with a slash and dots) that marks the point in the music where a performer should return to when instructed by a [[D. S. al Coda]] or D.S. al Fine direction.

It acts as a bookmark, indicating the starting point for a repeat or a jump back in the song's structure.

**Pro-Tip:** Always clearly identify the [[Dal Segno (D.S.)]] sign on your chart. It's the anchor point for navigating repeats. When you see a "D.S." instruction, your eyes should immediately jump back to this symbol to continue playing from there.
