# Linkin Park
`[[Linkin Park]]` was an American rock band formed in Agoura Hills, California, in 1996. They rose to prominence in the early 2000s as a leading act in the `[[Nu Metal]]` and `[[Alternative Rock]]` genres. Their sound is a prime example of the fusion discussed in `[[Intro to Two-Handed 16ths]]`, combining aggressive `[[Heavy Metal]]` guitar riffs with electronic textures, hip-hop influences, and powerful, often `[[Syncopated Beats]]`. Their drumming frequently features driving `[[Two-Handed 16th-Note Grooves]]` and dynamic `[[Fills]]` that contribute significantly to their impactful `[[Rhythmic Textures]]` within `[[Rock Music Styles]]`.
