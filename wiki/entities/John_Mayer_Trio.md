# John Mayer Trio
The [[John Mayer Trio]] is a highly acclaimed musical group known for its blend of [[Blues]], [[Rock]], and [[Funk]] influences. Comprising John Mayer on guitar and vocals, Pino Palladino on bass, and [[Steve Jordan]] on drums, the trio is celebrated for its exceptional musicianship, improvisational prowess, and deep grooves.

**Significance in Funk-Rock:**
*   **Modern Standard:** The trio is cited as a modern example in the tradition of [[Funk-Rock]], showcasing how contemporary artists continue to evolve the genre with sophistication and feel.
*   **Groove-Oriented:** Their music is heavily groove-oriented, with a strong emphasis on the rhythmic interplay between the three members.
*   **Drumming by Steve Jordan:** The drumming of [[Steve Jordan]] is a cornerstone of the trio's sound, characterized by his impeccable pocket, tasteful [[Syncopation]], and deep understanding of [[Funk]] and [[Blues]] drumming.

**Pro-Tip for Drummers:** Study the live performances and recordings of the [[John Mayer Trio]]. Pay close attention to [[Steve Jordan]]'s approach to [[Groove]], his use of [[Ghost Notes]], [[Rimshot]]s, and how he supports the guitar and bass. His drumming is a masterclass in musicality and restraint, proving that it's not always about playing more, but playing *right*.
