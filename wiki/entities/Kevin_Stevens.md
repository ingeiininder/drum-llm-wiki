# Kevin Stevens

**Kevin Stevens** is a Los Angeles-based drummer and educator known for his deep focus on [[Groove]] and historical drumming styles.

### Professional Experience
*   **Television**: Performed with the house band for *Jimmy Kimmel Live*.
*   **Touring**: Has toured with Shakira, Beto Cuevas, and Minnie Driver.
*   **Theater**: Original cast member of the Broadway musical *Rock of Ages*.
*   **Ensembles**: Founder of the *Brass Monkey Brass Band*.

### Educational Contributions
At Musicians Institute, Stevens is a specialist in "getting your groove together." He created the **New Orleans Drumming** class, which explores the history of the drum set and its role in jazz, R&B, and funk.
