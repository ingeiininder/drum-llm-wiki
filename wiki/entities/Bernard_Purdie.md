# Bernard Purdie
**Bernard "Pretty" Purdie** is one of the most recorded session drummers in history and is the architect of the **Purdie Shuffle**, a definitive [[Half_Time_Shuffle]] groove.

### Contributions to Drumming
- **The Purdie Shuffle**: A ghost-note heavy, triplet-based groove that has influenced countless genres, from rock to hip-hop.
- **Feel and Ghosting**: Purdie's mastery of internal dynamics and "the space between the notes" set the standard for modern session playing.
- **Influence**: His work provided the blueprint for the grooves found in [[Indie_Pop_Drumming]] and the technical exercises used to develop [[Half_Time_Shuffle_Feel]].
