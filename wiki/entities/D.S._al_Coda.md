# D.S. al Coda
"D.S. al Coda" is a musical notation instruction that stands for "Dal Segno al Coda," meaning "from the sign to the coda." When encountered, the performer should:
1.  Go back to the "Segno" sign (𝄋).
2.  Play from the Segno until reaching the "To Coda" (𝄌) sign.
3.  Then, skip directly to the "Coda" section (𝄌 Coda).

This instruction is a common way to navigate [[Song Form]]s, allowing for repetition of sections while providing a clear path to the ending.

**Pro-Tip:** Always be aware of navigation markings like D.S. al Coda, D.C. al Fine, etc., especially in live performance. A quick glance ahead can prevent missed cues or getting lost in the form. Practice sight-reading charts with these markings to build confidence.
