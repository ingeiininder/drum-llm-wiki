# Jason McGerr
**Jason McGerr** is a contemporary drummer best known for his work with the band **Death Cab for Cutie**. He is considered a "groove-master" in the [[Indie_Pop_Drumming]] genre.

### Musical Style
- **Technical Precision**: McGerr is known for incorporating complex rudimental ideas and odd subdivisions (like [[16th-Note Triplets]]) into accessible pop/rock contexts.
- **Compositional Drumming**: His parts are often highly orchestrated and integral to the song's melodic structure, avoiding "wasted space."
- **Educational Impact**: His approach to the [[Half_Time_Shuffle_Feel]] is a primary study point for students learning modern indie-pop performance.
