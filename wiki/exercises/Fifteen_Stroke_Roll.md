# Fifteen-Stroke Roll

The **Fifteen-Stroke Roll** is a long-duration rudiment that, like the eleven-stroke roll, does **not** naturally alternate.

### Structure and Sticking
- **Composition**: Seven sets of doubles followed by one single-stroke accent.
- **Hand Motions**: Eight hand motions.
- **Sticking Pattern**: `R R L L R R L L R R L L R R L`.

### Practice Method
- **One Way / Other Way**: Practice starting exclusively with the right hand for several repetitions, then switch to the left hand.
- **Alternating**: To practice "both ways," ensure you are mentally prepared for the lead hand to stay the same unless a single stroke is added elsewhere in the bar.
