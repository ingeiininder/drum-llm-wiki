# Four-Beat Fill (Country-Pop Pre-Chorus Bar 4)
Bar 4 of the pre-chorus in the [[Country-Pop Play-Along Chart (Unit 5)]] offers an opportunity to create your own four-beat [[Fill]]. This encourages creativity and musicality within the song's structure.

**Creative Approach**:
*   **Plan of Attack**: Think ahead and have a clear "plan of attack" for your fill.
*   **Contextual Relevance**: Consider where the fill is leading. This will help you create a fill that is appropriate to the overall [[Country-Pop Style]] of the song.

**Pro-Tip**: A good fill doesn't just sound cool; it serves the song. Experiment with different rhythmic ideas, dynamics, and drum voicings (e.g., [[Snare Drum]], [[Toms]], [[Cymbals]]). Ensure your fill complements the song's energy and leads smoothly into the next section, rather than distracting from it. Consider using elements from the existing [[Grooves]] or [[Ensemble Figures]] to maintain stylistic consistency.
