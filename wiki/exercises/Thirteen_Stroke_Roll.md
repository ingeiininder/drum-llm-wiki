# Thirteen-Stroke Roll

The **Thirteen-Stroke Roll** is an alternating rudiment that provides a longer duration of sustain than the nine-stroke roll.

### Structure and Sticking
- **Composition**: Six sets of doubles followed by one single-stroke accent.
- **Hand Motions**: Seven hand motions (e.g., RR-LL-RR-LL-RR-LL-R).
- **Counting**: Count "1-2-3-4-5-6-7" to track the hand movements.

### Technical Focus
- **Sticking Pattern**: `R R L L R R L L R R L L R`.
- **Application**: Excellent for filling a half-note duration in a medium-tempo march or rock ballad.
