# Double Paradiddle Drumset Mobility

The **Double Paradiddle** (`R L R L R R / L R L R L L`) extends the standard paradiddle by adding an extra pair of single strokes. This makes it naturally fit into **6/8 time** or **16th-note triplet** subdivisions.

### Orchestration Guide
*   **Accented Pattern**: First, master the accented version on a single surface.
*   **Voicing**: Move the accented single strokes to the toms.
*   **Subdivisions**: Practice as both **16th-note triplets** (where the rudiment fits perfectly into two beats) and **straight 16th notes** (which creates a cross-rhythm/over-the-barline feel).

### Professional Context
The double paradiddle is excellent for creating long, flowing fills that move across the entire kit. Because of the extra singles, you have more time to reach distant drums or cymbals compared to the standard paradiddle.
