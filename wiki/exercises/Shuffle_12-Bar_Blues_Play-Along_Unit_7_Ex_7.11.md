# Shuffle 12-Bar Blues Play-Along (Unit 7 Ex 7.11)
This exercise focuses on applying [[Shuffle]] grooves within a standard [[12-Bar Blues]] progression. The tempo is set at a [[Swing]] feel of J = 120 BPM.

The chart includes specific sections and instructions, such as "Take Coda on cue," indicating a navigation point within the musical form. Drummers should be prepared to transition smoothly to the [[Coda]] when prompted.

**Pro-Tip:** When playing [[Shuffle]] grooves, pay close attention to the triplet subdivision to ensure an authentic [[Swing]] feel. Practice the [[Groove_#1_Four_on_the_Floor_Shuffle]] and [[Groove_#2_Syncopated_Shuffle]] independently before integrating them into the full [[12-Bar Blues]] form. Focus on the interaction between the [[Bass_Drum]], [[Snare_Drum]], and [[Hi-Hat]] to create a cohesive and driving rhythm.
