# Whole Notes, Half Notes and Quarter Notes with Accompanying Rests (Exercises)
This set of exercises focuses on developing proficiency with the fundamental [[Rhythmic_Values]] of [[Whole_Note]]s, [[Half_Note]]s, and [[Quarter_Note]]s, along with their corresponding [[Whole_Rest]]s, [[Half_Rest]]s, and [[Quarter_Rest]]s. These exercises are designed to be played on a single surface (e.g., [[Practice Pad]] or [[Snare_Drum]]) at a starting [[Beats_Per_Minute_(BPM)|BPM]] of 75, repeating each exercise ten times.

## A. Whole Note Exercises
*   **Objective**: To solidify the understanding and execution of [[Whole_Note]]s and [[Whole_Rest]]s.
*   **Description**: These exercises typically involve playing a single [[Whole_Note]] per [[Measure]] or observing a [[Whole_Rest]] for a full measure.
*   **Pro-Tip**: Focus on a strong, clear stroke on [[Beat]] 1 for the whole note, and maintain a steady internal [[Counting_Beats|count]] through the entire [[Whole_Rest]].

## B. Half Note Exercises
*   **Objective**: To develop accuracy with [[Half_Note]]s and [[Half_Rest]]s.
*   **Description**: These exercises will feature two [[Half_Note]]s per measure (on beats 1 and 3) or two [[Half_Rest]]s, or a combination (e.g., a half note followed by a half rest).
*   **Pro-Tip**: Ensure the two-beat duration of the half note is consistent, and the silence of the half rest is precisely two beats.

## C. Whole Note and Half Note Combination Exercises
*   **Objective**: To practice transitioning smoothly between [[Whole_Note]]s and [[Half_Note]]s (and their rests).
*   **Description**: These exercises will combine whole notes and half notes within and across measures, requiring the drummer to switch between four-beat and two-beat durations.
*   **Pro-Tip**: Pay close attention to the start of each note/rest. The transition from a four-beat duration to a two-beat duration, or vice-versa, needs to be seamless.

## D. Quarter Note Exercises
*   **Objective**: To establish a solid and consistent [[Quarter_Note]] pulse.
*   **Description**: These exercises will primarily consist of continuous [[Quarter_Note]]s, often with interspersed [[Quarter_Rest]]s.
*   **Pro-Tip**: Focus on evenness of strokes and dynamics. The quarter note is the backbone of many grooves, so a rock-solid quarter note pulse is essential. Ensure the quarter rests are sharp and precisely one beat long.

## General Practice Advice
*   **Metronome Use**: Always use a [[Metronome]] to ensure accurate timing.
*   **Vocalization**: Continue to vocalize the counts (1, 2, 3, 4) as you play.
*   **Listen**: Pay attention to the sound of your strokes and the accuracy of your rests.
