# Dotted-Half-Note Click (3/4 Time)
This is `[[Method 2]]` for `[[Practicing_3_4_Time]]` using a `[[Metronome]]`. This method challenges your `[[Internal Pulse]]` by providing fewer clicks.

**Method:**
1.  Set your `[[Metronome]]` to 40 `[[BPM]]`.
2.  Ensure there are no `[[Accented Clicks]]`.
3.  Treat each click as a `[[Dotted Half Note]]`. (A dotted half note in 3/4 time equals three quarter notes, or one full measure).
4.  Play the same groove as in `[[Quarter-Note_Click_3_4_Time]]` (e.g., `[[Ex 9.1]]`).

**Pro-Tip:** This method forces you to feel the entire `[[3_4_Time_Signature]]` measure as a single unit, rather than relying on individual quarter-note clicks. It's excellent for developing a strong `[[Internal Clock]]` and preventing `[[Rushing Tempo]]`. If you find yourself struggling, slow down even further or temporarily switch back to the `[[Quarter-Note_Click_3_4_Time]]` method to re-establish your feel, then return to the dotted-half-note click.
