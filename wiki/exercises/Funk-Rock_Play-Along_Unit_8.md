# Funk-Rock Play-Along (Unit 8)
This `[[Funk-Rock Play-Along]]` is the culminating exercise for Unit 8, Chapter 3, designed to integrate all the `[[Groove]]` and `[[Musical Form]]` concepts learned. It features various sections, including a `[[Guitar Introduction (Funk-Rock)]]`, `[[Snare Drum Pick-Up Fill (Funk-Rock)]]`, `[[Letter A Groove (Funk-Rock)]]`, `[[Letter B Groove (Funk-Rock)]]`, and navigation via `[[D.C. al Coda (No Repeats)]]`.

The play-along likely includes specific cues like "One," "Two," "Three," "Four" for counting in, and instructions for `[[Full Band Entrance]]`. The musical examples `[[Ex 8.13]]` provide the full chart context.

**Pro-Tip:** Approach a full play-along by first practicing each section individually until proficient. Then, practice transitions between sections. Pay close attention to `[[Dynamics]]`, `[[Articulation]]`, and `[[Timekeeping]]` throughout. The goal is not just to play the notes correctly, but to perform with the appropriate `[[Funk-Rock]]` feel and interact musically with the backing track. Use `[[Recording Yourself]]` as a tool to refine your performance.
