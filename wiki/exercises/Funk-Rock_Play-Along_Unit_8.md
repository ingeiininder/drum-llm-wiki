This `[[Funk-Rock Play-Along]]` is the culminating exercise for Unit 8, Chapter 3, designed to integrate all the `[[Groove]]` and `[[Musical Form]]` concepts learned. It features various sections, including a `[[Guitar_Introduction_Funk-Rock]]`, `[[Snare_Drum_Pick-Up_Fill_Funk-Rock]]`, `[[Letter_A_Groove_Funk-Rock]]`, `[[Letter_B_Groove_Funk-Rock]]`, and navigation via `[[D.C._al_Coda_No_Repeats]]`.

**Pro-Tip:** Approach a full play-along by first practicing each section individually until proficient. Then, practice transitions between sections. Pay close attention to `[[Dynamics]]`, `[[Articulations]]`, and `[[Timekeeping]]` throughout. The goal is not just to play the notes correctly, but to perform with the appropriate `[[Funk-Rock]]` feel and interact musically with the backing track.
