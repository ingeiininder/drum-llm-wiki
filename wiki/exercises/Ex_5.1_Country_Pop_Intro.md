# Ex 5.1 Country Pop Intro
This exercise, Ex 5.1, focuses on the [[Song_Intro]] for a [[Country-Pop_Genre]] tune at a tempo of J=98 BPM. The intro is four bars long and features a [[Pre-Programmed_Drum_Loop]] that runs continuously. A guitar lick enters on bars 3-4, providing an additional musical element to lock into.

Students are expected to understand how to integrate their playing with a pre-existing loop and anticipate the entrance of other instruments, setting the stage for the rest of the song.

**Pro-Tip:** Pay close attention to the interaction between the drum loop and the guitar entrance. Your role is to support these elements, ensuring a smooth and cohesive start to the song. Practice counting in accurately and feeling the pulse established by the loop.
