# Tenuto Interpretation
**Tenuto** markings indicate that a note should be held for its full value. In drum set terms, this translates to "long" sounds.

### Performance Characteristics
*   **Set-up Fills**: These can be larger and more melodic than staccato set-ups, leading the listener into the sustained sound.
*   **Orchestration**: Primarily played on **Crash Cymbals** supported by the **Bass Drum**.
*   **Clarity vs. Length**: For a string of eighth notes marked tenuto, playing them on the **Snare Drum** paired with the **Ride Cymbal** allows the figure to have rhythmic clarity while maintaining a "shimmer" of length.

**Pro-Tip**: Use larger cymbals (e.g., 18" or 19" crashes) for tenuto figures to ensure the decay lasts until the next musical event.
