# Homework Assignments (IMG_0556)
This section outlines the required homework for the current lesson in [[Drum_Technique_50]]. Students are expected to demonstrate proficiency in several key areas:

*   **Roll Mastery**: Be able to execute all fundamental [[Drum Rolls]] and their respective [[Roll_Variations]]. This includes understanding the mechanics and musical application of each.
*   **Master Studies Application**: Successfully perform [[Master_Studies_Exercises_13-24]] from pages 14 and 15 of the [[Master_Studies_Book]]. Focus should be on precision, dynamics, and musicality.
*   **Coordination and Balance**: Execute the designated [[Bass_Drum_and_Hi-Hat_Coordination_Exercise]] with complete independence and without relying on external support for balance. This emphasizes core stability and control.

**Pro-Tip**: Approach homework as a performance, not just practice. Record yourself to objectively assess your execution and identify areas for improvement.
**Common Pitfall**: Neglecting the "without holding on to anything for balance" aspect of the coordination exercise. This indicates a lack of core strength or proper [[Seated_Balance_Technique_for_Hi-Hat_and_Bass_Drum]].
