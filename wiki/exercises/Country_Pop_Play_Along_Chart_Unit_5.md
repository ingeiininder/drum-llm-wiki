# Country-Pop Play-Along Chart (Unit 5)
This entry refers to the full drum chart for the "Country-Pop Play-Along" exercise in Unit 5, Chapter 3. It's designed to help drummers apply the concepts and techniques learned in the unit within a musical context.

**Key Elements**:
*   **Tempo**: 98 BPM.
*   **Structure**: Includes sections like A, B, pre-chorus, and a [[Coda]].
*   **Grooves**: Features various [[Grooves]] on [[Tight Hi-Hat]], [[Sloshy Hi-Hat]], and [[Ride Cymbal]].
*   **Fills**: Incorporates specific [[Fill]]s, such as the [[One-Beat Fill (Country-Pop A Section Bar 8)]], [[Four-Beat Fill (Country-Pop Pre-Chorus Bar 4)]], and [[Two-Beat Fill (Country-Pop B Section First Ending)]].

**Practice Suggestions**:
*   Practice the chart grooves without the play-along track at varying [[Tempo]]s (80, 90, 98 BPMs) using a [[Metronome]].
*   Play along to the track twice a day for a total of eight minutes.
*   Focus on developing a [[Developing Stylistic Drum Sound|stylistically correct drum sound]] by [[Recording Practice Sessions|recording yourself]].
