# Two-Beat Fill (Country-Pop B Section First Ending)
Within the first ending of the B section in the [[Country_Pop_Play_Along_Chart_Unit_5]], there is a direction to play a two-beat [[Fill]].

**Musical Context**:
*   This fill's primary purpose is to lead the song back to a repeat of the B section.
*   Given its function as a transition back to a repeated section, it is appropriate to keep this fill on the subtle side.

**Pro-Tip**: Avoid overly complex or loud fills here. A simple, tasteful fill that maintains the song's flow is often more effective. Consider using a [[Snare Drum]] or [[Hi-Hat]] based pattern that doesn't draw too much attention but smoothly guides the listener back to the beginning of the B section.
