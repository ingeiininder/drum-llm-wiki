# Staccato Interpretation
When interpreting **Staccato** markings in an [[Ensemble Figures]] context, the goal is brevity and precision.

### Performance Characteristics
*   **Set-up Fills**: Should be short and simple. Avoid long rolls or complex flams that might bleed into the figure.
*   **Orchestration**: Figures are typically played on the **Snare Drum** or **Bass Drum**. 
*   **Hi-Hat Integration**: Pairing the figure with a tight hi-hat "chick" helps define the short duration.
*   **Note Length**: There should be immediate silence following the stroke.

**Common Pitfall**: Over-playing the fill. A staccato figure needs a "clean" entrance to maintain its "short" character.
