# Inward Paradiddle

The **Inward Paradiddle** (also known as the **Inverted Paradiddle**) uses the sticking:
`R L L R L R R L`

### Characteristics
Unlike the standard paradiddle, the double strokes in the inward variation occur on the "inside" of the pattern (beats 2-3 and 6-7). This creates a different rhythmic flow and weight, often feeling more "driven" than the standard version.

### Drumset Application
*   **16th Note Orchestration**: Move the single strokes (1 and 4) to the toms while keeping the doubles (2 and 3) on the snare.
*   **16th Triplet Orchestration**: Applying this sticking to a triplet grid creates a sophisticated polyrhythmic feel.
