# Metronome Practice (Unit 4)
A core component of [[Practice Suggestions (Unit 4)]] is dedicated [[Metronome]] practice. Students are advised to:
*   Practice the [[Chart Grooves]] from the unit *without* the [[Play-Along Track Practice]].
*   Use a metronome at specific tempos: 85 BPM, 100 BPM, and 110 BPM.
*   Allocate twenty minutes daily to this exercise.

This structured approach helps develop internal clock, rhythmic precision, and consistency across different tempos, which are crucial for any [[Drum Performance]].

**Pro-Tip:** Don't just play *to* the metronome; try to play *with* it, making the click disappear into your groove. Start at a comfortable tempo and gradually increase it. If you struggle at a higher tempo, slow it down until you can play it perfectly, then incrementally increase again. This builds solid rhythmic foundations.
