# The A Groove (Eighth-Note Rock Groove)
"The A Groove" is an eighth-note rock groove found within Letter A of the chart, widely used in popular recordings. It combines familiar [[Hi-Hat]] and [[Snare_Drum]] elements with a new [[Bass_Drum]] pattern.

A key feature of this groove is the [[Hi-Hat]] employing an accent on every quarter note (beats 1, 2, 3, and 4).

## Performance Notes
It is crucial to ensure that each limb lines up precisely. Whenever a [[Snare_Drum]] or [[Bass_Drum]] hit coincides with a [[Hi-Hat]] hit, they should be played in unison, with no [[Flams]] present. Dedicated practice time is essential to refine this approach for proper feel and sound.
