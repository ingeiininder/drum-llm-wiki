# Tied Half Notes and Quarter Notes (Two-Bar Phrases) - Exercise B
This exercise specifically targets the reading and performance of [[Ties]] involving [[Half_Note]] and [[Quarter Notes]] within two-bar phrases. It reinforces the concept of extended [[Duration]] and the importance of accurate [[Sustain]] across measures.

**Key Learning Points:**
*   **Combined Values**: Accurately calculate and feel the combined duration of tied half notes and quarter notes.
*   **Bar Line Transitions**: Practice smooth transitions when ties extend across the [[Bar Line]], maintaining the rhythmic pulse.
*   **Rhythmic Precision**: Ensure that the single attack for tied notes is precisely timed, and the subsequent duration is accurately felt.

**Pro-Tip:** Focus on the [[2_3_Rule]] when applicable, especially if half notes are tied to quarter notes to clarify the [[Downbeat]] of beat 3. Even if the notation doesn't strictly follow it, understanding the principle helps in interpreting the intended rhythm.
