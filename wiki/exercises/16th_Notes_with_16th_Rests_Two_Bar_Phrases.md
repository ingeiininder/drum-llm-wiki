# 16th Notes with 16th Rests (Two Bar Phrases)
This series of exercises (B through J) extends the focus on [[Sixteenth Notes]] and [[16th Rests]] into two-bar phrases. This progression builds endurance, rhythmic continuity, and the ability to maintain a precise internal clock over longer musical statements.

**Practice Guidance:**
1.  **Flow and Continuity:** The challenge here is to maintain the same level of precision and accuracy across two full measures. Avoid rushing or dragging at the bar line.
2.  **Breathing/Phrasing:** Think of the two-bar phrase as a complete musical idea. This can help with maintaining a consistent feel.
3.  **Memory and Anticipation:** As the phrases are longer, you'll start to develop a sense of anticipation for upcoming patterns, which is crucial for sight-reading.

**Pro-Tip:** These exercises are excellent for developing your ability to play longer [[Drum Fills]] or maintain intricate [[Grooves]] without losing your place. Practice connecting the end of the first bar seamlessly to the beginning of the second.
