# Stab (Track)

"Stab" is a funk-oriented track by Jeff Salem, Josh Harris, and Dave DiMarco. It emphasizes the "Question and Answer" interplay between the bass guitar and the horn section.

### Performance Guidelines
- **Interplay**: The drummer must compliment the horn parts without "cluttering" them.
- **Sectional Figures**: Catching horn figures in Section A while maintaining a steady, driving groove.
- **Syncopation**: Section A ends with a highly syncopated bass line that requires the drummer to either "comp" (complement) the rhythm or hold a rock-solid pulse against it.

### Technical Focus
- **Fill Choice**: Fills should be selected based on how they sit with the bass and horn lines. One-bar and two-bar fill ideas are recommended.
- **Odd Phrasing**: Note that Section B2 is 10 bars long and Section C is 6 bars long, breaking the standard 4/8 bar symmetry.

### Groove Variations
The curriculum provides three levels of grooves for this track:
- **Easy**: Basic time-keeping focusing on the backbeat.
- **Intermediate**: Incorporating more syncopated bass drum patterns.
- **Advanced**: High-density ghost notes and complex [[Linear Phrasing]].

**Pro-Tip**: Pay close attention to the "Bass Lick" at the end of Section E. You have the choice to either play in unison with it or stay out of the way—both are valid professional choices depending on the desired energy.
