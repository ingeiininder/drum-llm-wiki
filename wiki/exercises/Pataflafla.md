# Pataflafla
The **Pataflafla** is a four-note rudiment characterized by flams on the first and fourth notes (lR L R rL). It is a powerful tool for creating syncopated, driving rhythms on the drumset.

### Orchestration Variations
- **16th-Note Orchestration**: Spreading the four notes across the snare and toms in a linear fashion.
- **Eighth-Note Triplet Orchestration**: Adapting the rudiment to a triplet feel, which changes the "swing" of the flams.

### Technical Focus
- Ensure the two inner single strokes (L R) are played at a consistent height and volume.
- The "outer" flams should provide a rhythmic "frame" for the exercise.
