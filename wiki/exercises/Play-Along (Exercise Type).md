# Play-Along (Exercise Type)
A "[[Play-Along]]" is a common exercise type featured in each unit of "[[Drum Performance 1 (Book)]]", allowing drummers to practice the learned styles and techniques with accompanying music. Examples include the [[Pop-Rock (Drumming Style)|Pop-Rock Play-Along]] and [[Hard Rock (Drumming Style)|Hard Rock Play-Along]].
