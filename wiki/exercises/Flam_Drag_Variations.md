# Flam Drag Variations
These hybrid rudiments combine the [[Flams]] and the [[Drag]], requiring high-level finger control and wrist isolation.

### Double Flam Drag (Ex 5.8)
A combination where the drag is nested within a flam-accent-style movement. It challenges the player to maintain the "up-down" stroke motion while inserting double strokes.

### One-Hand Flam Drag (Ex 5.9)
An advanced coordination exercise where the flam and the subsequent drag are executed with a focus on one-hand lead stability.
- **Sticking Pattern**: R R L rL R R L rL
- **Pro-Tip**: Use a [[Mirror]] to monitor stick heights. The grace note of the flam should be significantly lower than the primary stroke to ensure proper [[Articulations]].

### Application
These rudiments are frequently used in modern drum corps and "grid" variations to increase technical facility.
