# 16ths (Two-Bar Phrases) - 2/4

This exercise extends the practice of [[Sixteenth Note]]s into two-bar phrases within the [[2/4 Time Signature]]. This helps drummers develop endurance and continuity when performing sixteenth note-based rhythms over longer periods.

**Purpose:**
*   To reinforce the accurate execution of sixteenth notes in a continuous context.
*   To develop rhythmic endurance and the ability to sustain focus over two-bar phrases.
*   To improve [[Sight Reading]] for extended sixteenth note passages.

**Pro-Tips:**
*   **Flow:** Focus on maintaining a smooth, continuous flow of sixteenth notes across the bar line.
*   **Look Ahead:** Practice reading ahead to anticipate upcoming sixteenth note patterns and ensure a seamless transition between measures.
*   **Internal Clock:** Rely on your internal [[Pulse]] and consistent subdivision to keep the sixteenth notes even, even when the patterns change.
*   **Common Pitfall:** Breaking the flow of sixteenth notes at the bar line or when transitioning between different rhythmic figures.

Sustaining sixteenth note accuracy over multiple bars is a key step towards rhythmic fluency and precision.
