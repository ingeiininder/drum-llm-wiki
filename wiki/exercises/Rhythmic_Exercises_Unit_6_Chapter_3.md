# Rhythmic Exercises: Unit 6, Chapter 3
This chapter presents a comprehensive set of one-bar rhythmic exercises designed to reinforce [[Musical_Note_Values]], [[Rhythmic Groupings]], and [[Common_Eighth_and_16th_Pairings_Rhythmic_Groupings]] from previous units. Crucially, these exercises are intended to be practiced with the [[Sticking_Patterns]] introduced in Unit 6, specifically [[Assigned_Sticking]] and [[Double_Strokes]].

The exercises cover a wide range of rhythmic combinations, including:
*   **[[16th_Note]]s, [[Eighth_Note]]s, and [[Quarter_Note]]s (One-Bar Phrases)**: Integrating various note durations.
*   **[[16th_Note]]s and [[Eighth_Note]]s (One-Bar Phrases)**: Focusing on common subdivisions.
*   **[[16th_Note]]s and [[Eighth_Note]]s with [[Eighth_Rest]]s (One-Bar Phrases)**: Incorporating short silences for precision.
*   **[[16th_Note]]s, [[Eighth_Note]]s, and [[Quarter_Note]]s with [[Quarter_Rest]]s (One-Bar Phrases)**: Combining longer rests with varied note values.
*   **[[16th_Note]]s, [[Eighth_Note]]s, and [[Quarter_Note]]s with [[Eighth_Rest]]s (One-Bar Phrases)**: More complex patterns with mixed rests.
*   **[[16th_Note]]s, [[Eighth_Note]]s, and [[Quarter_Note]]s with [[Eighth_Rest]]s and [[Quarter_Rest]]s (One-Bar Phrases)**: The most comprehensive exercises, challenging the drummer with a full range of note and rest values.

**Pro-Tip**: Approach these exercises with "great detail and care." The goal is not just to play the rhythms, but to apply the learned [[Sticking_Techniques_General]] consistently and accurately. This will solidify your [[Drum_Reading]] skills and build robust [[Muscle_Memory_Drumming]], preparing you for more embellished and combined groupings in future studies. Use a metronome and practice slowly to ensure precision before increasing tempo.
