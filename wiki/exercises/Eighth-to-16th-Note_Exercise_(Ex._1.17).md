## Purpose:
*   **Dynamic Control**: Practicing the distinction between accented (down stroke) and unaccented notes.
*   **Stroke Type Application**: Reinforcing the proper execution of the [[Down Stroke]] and subsequent [[Tap]]s or free strokes.
*   **Rhythmic Precision**: Developing accuracy when playing eighth and 16th note subdivisions.
*   **Grip Familiarization**: Used to become familiar with different grip positions, such as [[American Grip]] and [[German Grip]], and different [[Fulcrum]] types like the [[Second-Finger Fulcrum]].

## Practice Suggestions:
*   **Start Slow**: Begin at a slow tempo to ensure precision and proper stroke mechanics.
*   **Grip Experimentation**: Practice the exercise first with [[American Grip]], then experiment with [[German Grip]], and potentially other grips, paying attention to how each grip influences the feel and sound.
*   **Fulcrum Focus**: When using the [[Second-Finger Fulcrum]], concentrate on how it affects power and the feel of the stick.
*   **Consistency**: Aim for consistent dynamics and timing for all notes, especially the accented down strokes.
*   **Mirror/Video**: Use a mirror or video recording to visually check your stick heights and hand movements.
