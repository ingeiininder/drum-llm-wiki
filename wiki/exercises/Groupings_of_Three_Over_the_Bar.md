# Groupings of Three (Over-the-Bar)

This exercise focuses on patterns that repeat every three units of a specific subdivision, creating a cross-rhythm against a standard 4/4 meter.

### 1. Every Third Quarter Note (Ex 9.1)
- **Value**: Dotted Half Note.
- **Pattern**: Accenting every 3rd beat. 
- **Cycle**: In 4/4 time, the accent will land on:
  - Bar 1: Beat **1**, Beat **4**
  - Bar 2: Beat **3**
  - Bar 3: Beat **2**
  - Bar 4: Beat **1** (The cycle resets)

### 2. Every Third 16th Note (Ex 9.2)
- **Value**: Dotted Eighth Note.
- **Pattern**: Accenting every 3rd 16th note.
- **Sticking**: Using [[Hand-to-Hand Sticking]] (R-L-R-L), the accents will alternate between the Right and Left hands.
- **Musical Effect**: This creates a "secondary pulse" that feels like a faster tempo superimposed over the original beat.

### Practice Goal
Count the bars (1, 2, 3, 4) out loud while playing the accents. Do not let the accents pull your "internal clock" away from the downbeat of the measure.
