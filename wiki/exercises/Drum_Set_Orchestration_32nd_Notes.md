# Drum Set Orchestration (32nd Notes)

Once the concept of [[32nd Note Interpretation]] is mastered on the snare drum, it should be orchestrated across the drum set to enhance [[Phrasing]].

### Orchestration Methods
1.  **Bass Drum Support**: Add the bass drum to every accented note within the 32nd-note stream.
2.  **Tom Movement**: Move the accented notes to various toms while keeping the unaccented "filler" 32nd notes on the snare.
3.  **Cymbal Accents**: Play the accented figures on the crash or ride cymbals (supported by the bass drum) while maintaining the 32nd-note flow on the snare.
4.  **Hi-Hat Integration**: Play the entire 32nd-note pattern on the hi-hat, using the bass drum to highlight the written ensemble figures.

### Goal
The objective is to move seamlessly between the "grid" of 32nd notes and the specific rhythmic requirements of the chart.
