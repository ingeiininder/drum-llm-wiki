# Quarter Notes

A **Quarter Note** (or crotchet) is a musical note played for one quarter of the [[Duration|duration]] of a [[Whole_Note]].

## Characteristics
- **Notation**: Drawn with a filled-in oval [[Note_Head|note head]] and a straight, flagless stem.
- **Value**: In standard [[4-4_Time_Signature]], a quarter note lasts for exactly one [[Beat]]. 

## Role in Percussion
The quarter note often represents the fundamental [[Pulse|pulse]] of a song. The [[Bass_Drum]] or [[Snare_Drum]] may be played on quarter notes to anchor the [[Groove]].
