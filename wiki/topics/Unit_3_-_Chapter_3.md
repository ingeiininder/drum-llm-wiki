# Unit 3 - Chapter 3
This chapter focuses on developing control and musicality with `[[One-Handed 16th-Note]]` patterns, primarily through `[[Play-Along Tracks]]`. It builds foundational skills for executing faster and more intricate rhythms with a single hand on the hi-hat or ride cymbal, a crucial technique for various contemporary drumming styles. The chapter includes specific exercises like `[[Ex 3.20]]` and general `[[Practice Suggestions]]` to reinforce learning.
