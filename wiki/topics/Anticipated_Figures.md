# Anticipated Figures (Pushes)

**Anticipated Figures**, commonly referred to as **"Pushes,"** occur when the band plays a chord or rhythmic hit slightly before the expected downbeat—usually on the "ah" of 4 or the "&" of 4.

### Musical Effect
Pushes create a sense of forward momentum and "lift" in a song. In the context of [[Swung_16th_Phrasing]], [[The_Push|the push]] on the "ah" of 4 is particularly syncopated.

### Performance Tip
When playing a push on the "ah" of 4:
- Use the **left hand** for the crash cymbal.
- Maintain the [[Hi-Hat|hi-hat]] or ride pattern with the **right hand** to ensure the time does not drop or waver during the [[Syncopation|syncopation]].
