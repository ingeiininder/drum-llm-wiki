# Multiple Bar Phrases
[[Multiple Bar Phrases]] refer to musical ideas or rhythmic patterns that extend coherently over several measures, rather than just one or two. This concept encourages drummers to think beyond single-bar loops and to develop longer, more flowing rhythmic statements.

**Pro-Tip:** Drummers should strive to create [[Multiple Bar Phrases]] to build tension and release, add musical interest, and avoid sounding repetitive. This involves anticipating upcoming sections and crafting grooves and fills that lead smoothly from one phrase to the next. It's a key aspect of developing musicality and storytelling on the drums.
