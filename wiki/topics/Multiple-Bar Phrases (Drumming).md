# Multiple-Bar Phrases (Drumming)
This concept refers to drum patterns, particularly bass drum patterns, that extend over several measures before repeating, rather than repeating every single bar. For example, the [[A Section Groove (Unit 3)]] features a four-bar bass drum pattern, while the [[B Section Groove (Unit 3)]] has a two-bar bass drum pattern. This technique adds complexity and variation to the rhythmic structure of a piece.
