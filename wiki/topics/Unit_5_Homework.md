# Unit 5 Homework
This homework assignment focuses on solidifying [[Bass Drum Technique]], particularly dynamic control, and continuing the development of hand technique through [[Master Studies]] and [[Ruff_Rudiment]].

**Tasks:**
1.  **Bass Drum Dynamic Control Exercises:** Be able to play all of the [[Bass Drum Dynamic Control Exercises]] (Ex 5.3 - Ex 5.7).
2.  **Master Studies Exercises:** Be able to play exercises 25-48 on pages 9, 10, 11 and 12 of [[Master Studies]].
3.  **Ruff Rudiments Review:** Review all of the [[Ruff_Rudiment]] from [[Unit 4 Five-Stroke Ruff]] and [[Unit 4 Seven-Stroke Ruff]].

**Pro-Tip:**
When practicing the bass drum exercises, don't just focus on hitting the notes. Pay close attention to the *quality* of the sound and the dynamic contrast. For [[Master Studies]], maintain the same level of precision and musicality. The review of ruff rudiments is crucial for long-term retention and integration into your overall [[Drum_Technique_50]].
