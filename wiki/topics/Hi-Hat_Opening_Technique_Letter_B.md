# Hi-Hat Opening Technique (Letter B)
A specific [[Hi-Hat_Opening_Technique_Letter_B]] is employed in the 'B' section to enhance its energetic and driving feel. This technique involves a precise coordination between stick and foot:

*   **Opening:** The hi-hat is opened (and accented) on the "&" of beat 4 in the second measure of the phrase. This creates a distinct, shimmering accent.
*   **Closing:** Immediately afterward, the hi-hat is closed with the foot on beat 1 of the next bar.

This controlled opening and closing creates a dynamic burst that adds to the musical excitement and forward momentum of the 'B' section, contributing to the [[Slightly_Loose_Hi-Hat_Letter_B]] feel.

**Pro-Tip:** Precision in both opening and closing the hi-hat is crucial for making this articulation effective and clean. Practice the coordination between your stick hand and your hi-hat foot to ensure a tight and impactful sound. This technique is a powerful tool for [[Marking a Song's Form]].
