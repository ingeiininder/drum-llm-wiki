# [[Hi-Hat_Opening|Hi-Hat Opening]] Technique (Letter B)
A specific [[Hi-Hat_Opening_Technique_Letter_B]] is employed in the 'B' section to enhance its energetic and driving feel. This technique involves a precise coordination between stick and foot:

*   **Opening:** The [[Hi-Hat|hi-hat]] is opened (and accented) on the "&" of [[Beat|beat]] 4 in the second [[Measure|measure]] of the phrase. This creates a distinct, shimmering accent.
*   **Closing:** Immediately afterward, the [[Hi-Hat|hi-hat]] is closed with the foot on [[Beat|beat]] 1 of the next bar.

This controlled opening and closing creates a dynamic burst that adds to the musical excitement and forward momentum of the 'B' section, contributing to the [[Slightly_Loose_Hi-Hat_Letter_B]] feel.

**Pro-Tip:** Precision in both [[Opening_and_Closing_the_Hi-Hat|opening and closing the hi-hat]] is crucial for making this articulation effective and clean. Practice the coordination between your stick hand and your [[Hi-Hat|hi-hat]] foot to ensure a tight and impactful sound. This technique is a powerful tool for [[Marking_a_Song's_Form]].
