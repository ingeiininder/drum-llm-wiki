# [[Drum_Reading|Drum Reading]] 1 (MI Curriculum Series)
"[[Drum_Reading|Drum Reading]] 1" is a foundational instructional book within the [[Musicians_Institute]] Curriculum Series, specifically designed for the [[Drum_Program]]. It focuses on developing essential [[Music_Reading|music reading]] skills, technical proficiency, and performance understanding for drummers.
