# Floor Tom (Notation)
In [[Drum_Set_Notation_Key]], the [[Floor_Tom]] is notated on [[The_Staff]] to indicate its specific role and sound within a drum part.

## Standard Placement
*   The floor tom is represented by a standard solid black [[Note_Head]].
*   Its typical position is on the **first space below the staff** or the **lowest line** of the staff, placing it above the [[Bass_Drum_Notation]] but still in the lower register.

## Function in Notation
Floor tom notation is used to indicate fills, [[Accents|accents]], or specific rhythmic patterns that utilize the deep, resonant sound of the floor tom, often contributing to the [[Groove|groove]]'s weight or providing rhythmic punctuation.
