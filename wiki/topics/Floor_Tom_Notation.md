# Floor Tom (Notation)
In [[Drum Set Notation Key]], the [[Floor Tom]] is notated on [[The Staff]] to indicate its specific role and sound within a drum part.

## Standard Placement
*   The floor tom is represented by a standard solid black [[Note Head]].
*   Its typical position is on the **first space below the staff** or the **lowest line** of the staff, placing it above the [[Bass_Drum_Notation]] but still in the lower register.

## Function in Notation
Floor tom notation is used to indicate fills, accents, or specific rhythmic patterns that utilize the deep, resonant sound of the floor tom, often contributing to the groove's weight or providing rhythmic punctuation.
