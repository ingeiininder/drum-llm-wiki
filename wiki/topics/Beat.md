# Beat

The **Beat** is the basic unit of time, the [[Pulse|pulse]] (regularly repeating event), of the mensural level in musical rhythm. 

## Definition
It is the steady [[Pulse|pulse]] that you tap your foot to when listening to music. The speed of the beat is defined by the [[Tempo]].

## Relationship to Rhythm
While the beat is constant, the rhythm varies. Rhythms are constructed by dividing the beat into [[Subdivision]]s or extending notes across multiple beats.
