# The Count-Off
Often described as "the four most important notes that you will ever play," [[The Count-Off]] is the rhythmic cue given by the drummer (or bandleader) to establish the tempo, feel, and subdivision before a song begins. It is a critical act of communication that sets the stage for a tight and cohesive ensemble performance.

**Pro-Tip:** A clear, confident [[The Count-Off]] is vital for a strong start. Practice counting off in various tempos and feels (e.g., straight 8ths, swung 8ths, 16ths) to ensure you can effectively communicate the desired groove to the entire band. It's not just about the numbers; it's about the *feel*.
