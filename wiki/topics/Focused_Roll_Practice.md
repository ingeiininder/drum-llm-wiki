# Focused Roll Practice
This structured [[Practice_Routine]] for [[Drum_Rolls]] emphasizes targeted development of [[Roll_Rudiments]] and their [[Roll_Variations]]. The routine involves:

*   **Selection**: Choose two specific [[Roll_Rudiments]] (e.g., [[Five_Stroke_Roll]], [[Seven-Stroke_Roll]]) for the day's focus.
*   **Comprehensive Variation**: Play through all respective [[Roll_Variations]] for each chosen rudiment.
*   **Timed Intervals**: Dedicate two minutes to each selected roll, ensuring thorough exploration of its variations.
*   **Daily Alternation**: Alternate the two chosen rolls each day to ensure a balanced and comprehensive development across the entire spectrum of rolls.

This approach builds [[Endurance]], [[Speed]], and [[Control]] while preventing burnout on any single rudiment.

**Pro-Tip**: Use a [[Metronome]] to gradually increase [[Tempo|tempo]] and challenge your control at different speeds. Focus on the quality of the roll, not just the speed.
**Common Pitfall**: Practicing only the "easy" rolls or neglecting variations. True mastery requires addressing all aspects of each rudiment.
