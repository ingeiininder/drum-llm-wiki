# Tempo

**Tempo** refers to the speed or pace of a given piece of music. It is essential for determining the feel and forward momentum of a performance.

## Measurement
Tempo is typically measured in **BPM** (Beats Per Minute), indicating how many quarter notes (typically) occur within one minute of time.

## Tools
- **[[Metronome]]**: A device used to produce regular, metrical ticks to establish and maintain tempo.
- **Internal Clock**: A musician's developed sense of timing, crucial for maintaining a steady tempo without external aids.

## Consistency
Maintaining a steady tempo is one of the primary functions of a drummer in an ensemble, ensuring all musicians stay synchronized.
