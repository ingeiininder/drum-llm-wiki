# Letter B 16th-Note Ensemble Figure
Within the `[[Letter_B_Groove_Funk-Rock]]`, this [[Chart|chart]] includes specific `[[Ensemble_Figures]]` (often referred to as "hits") in the second bar of the [[Groove|groove]]. This particular figure is located on the "e" of [[Beat|beat]] 4 and is to be performed on both the `[[Bass_Drum]]` and `[[Crash_Cymbal]]`. Examples `[[Ex_8.8]]` and `[[Ex_8.9]]` illustrate this figure.

**Important:** As with other syncopated elements, `[[Subdividing]]` is crucial for accurately placing this figure. This prevents rushing the hit itself or the subsequent `[[Entrance]]` back into [[Beat|beat]] 1 of the next [[Measure|measure]].

**Pro-Tip:** `[[Ensemble_Hits]]` require precise `[[Timing]]` and `[[Dynamics]]` to lock in with the rest of the band. Practice hitting the `[[Crash_Cymbal]]` and `[[Bass_Drum]]` simultaneously with authority, ensuring the sound is unified and impactful. The "e" of [[Beat|beat]] 4 is a common `[[Syncopated]]` placement for hits, adding rhythmic excitement. Use your `[[Internal_Pulse]]` and `[[Subdivision]]` to nail the exact placement, avoiding the tendency to anticipate or delay the hit.
