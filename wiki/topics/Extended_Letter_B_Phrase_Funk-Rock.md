# Extended Letter B Phrase (Funk-Rock)
In the context of a [[Funk-Rock]] chart, particularly after a [[D.C._al_Coda_No_Repeats]], specific sections like "Letter B" can be intentionally extended. This [[Extended Letter B Phrase]] serves a distinct musical purpose: to create additional tension and act as a thematic link to the [[Coda]].

**Key Characteristics:**
*   **One Extra Bar:** The "Letter B" section is extended by one additional bar beyond its usual length.
*   **Purpose:** This extension is designed to "create additional tension within the chart."
*   **Thematic Connection:** The extra bar is "connected thematically to Letter B," meaning it maintains the musical character or motifs of the "Letter B" groove.
*   **Transition Fill:** Although technically part of the [[Coda]], this extra bar functions as an [[Extended_Phrase_Funk-Rock]] and a "ninth bar to Letter B," effectively serving as a transition fill leading into the main [[Coda]] section.

**Implications for Drummers:**
*   **Chart Awareness:** Drummers must be acutely aware of this specific extension, as it alters the expected phrase length. This is especially critical after navigating a [[D.C._al_Coda_No_Repeats]].
*   **Building Tension:** Your drumming in this extended bar should contribute to the build-up of tension. This might involve a slight dynamic increase, a specific rhythmic pattern, or a well-placed [[Fill]].
*   **Smooth Transition:** The extra bar acts as a bridge. Ensure a smooth and musical transition from the "Letter B" groove through this extended bar and into the subsequent [[Coda]].

**Pro-Tip:**
*   **Practice the Connection:** Focus on practicing the seamless connection between the standard "Letter B" groove, the extra bar, and the beginning of the [[Coda]].
*   **Dynamic Shaping:** Use dynamic control to build the tension effectively within this extended phrase.
*   **Rhythmic Continuity:** Maintain the stylistic integrity of the "Letter B" groove within the extended bar, even if it's functioning as a fill.
