# Right Hand Lead ([[Double_Strokes|Double Strokes]])
Right Hand Lead ([[Double_Strokes|Double Strokes]]) is a specific application of the [[Double_Strokes_Lead_Method]] where the right hand consistently initiates the double stroke pattern.

*   **Pattern**: When playing continuous [[16th_Note]]s, the sticking would be RR LL RR LL...
*   **Execution**: The right hand plays two consecutive strokes (RR), followed by the left hand playing two consecutive strokes (LL), and this sequence repeats.
*   **Purpose**: This method helps develop the right hand's ability to execute clean, even [[Double_Strokes|double strokes]] consistently.

**Pro-Tip**: Focus on the quality of the double stroke from the right hand. Ensure both 'R' strokes are even in volume and spacing. The second stroke often relies on rebound, so practice controlling that bounce. Equally important is the smooth transition to the left hand's double stroke.
