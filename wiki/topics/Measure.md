# Measure

A **Measure** (or Bar) is a segment of time defined by a given number of beats. 

## Demarcation
In music notation, measures are typically separated by vertical bar lines. The number of beats in a measure is dictated by the [[Time_Signature]].

## Function
Organizing music into measures helps musicians track their place in a composition and establishes the cyclical nature of musical [[Phrasing|phrasing]] and [[Groove]] patterns.
