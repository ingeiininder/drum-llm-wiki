# Quarter-Note [[Triplets|Triplets]]

Quarter-note [[Triplets|triplets]] (sometimes referred to as "half-note [[Triplets|triplets]]" in certain contexts, though they represent three notes over the span of two [[Quarter_Notes|quarter notes]]) are a subdivision that creates a mathematical "three-against-two" feel. 

## Derivation and Concept
Since two [[Eighth_Note]] equal one [[Quarter_Note]], within a triplet bracket, **two eighth-note [[Triplets|triplets]] are combined** to form a single quarter-note triplet. This combines longer note values with the inherent triplet feel.

### Mapping to the Eighth-Note Triplet Grid
The most accurate way to internalize quarter-note [[Triplets|triplets]] is to view them as every other note of an eighth-note triplet sequence:
- **Eighth-Note Triplet Grid**: 1 - trip - let - 2 - trip - let
- **Quarter-Note Triplet Placement**: **1** - (trip) - **let** - (2) - **trip** - (let)

Resulting placements:
1. The "1" ([[Beat|Beat]] 1)
2. The "let" of [[Beat|Beat]] 1
3. The "trip" of [[Beat|Beat]] 2

## Technical Execution
- **One-Handed Method**: When practicing alternating sticking (R-L-R L-R-L) for eighth-note [[Triplets|triplets]], the quarter-note [[Triplets|triplets]] will naturally fall on the **Right Hand** only. Removing the "filler" left hand strokes (Ex 2.4) reveals pure quarter-note [[Triplets|triplets]].
- **Accurate [[Duration|Duration]]**: On instruments without sustain (like drums), focus on the [[Duration|duration]] of silence between hits to ensure the "three-against-two" [[Pulse|pulse]] is even and not rushing.

## Combinations and [[Rests|Rests]]
Quarter-note [[Triplets|triplets]] are frequently mixed with eighth-note [[Triplets|triplets]] or [[Rests|rests]] to create complex [[Swing]] or [[Shuffle]] fragments:
- **Quarter + Eighth**: "1-trip-let" (where "1-trip" is a quarter note, "let" is an eighth).
- **Eighth + Quarter**: "1-trip-let" (where "1" is an eighth, "trip-let" is a quarter).
- **[[Rests|Rests]]**:
    - **Note - Rest - Note**
    - **Rest - Note - Note**
    - **Note - Note - Rest**
