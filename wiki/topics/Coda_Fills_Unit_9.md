# Coda Fills (Unit 9)
Within the [[Coda Section (Unit 9)]], the musical development includes the strategic addition of one-measure fills. These fills serve to build energy, provide rhythmic interest, and propel the song towards its conclusion.

**Role of the Fills:**
*   **Development**: These fills are not merely decorative but contribute to the evolving intensity of the coda. They break up the established patterns and introduce new rhythmic ideas.
*   **Energy Build-up**: By adding these one-measure fills, the piece gains momentum, leading into the final [[Coda Build-up (Unit 9)]].

**Performance Notes:**
*   **Integration**: Fills should sound like a natural extension of the groove, not an abrupt interruption. Practice the transitions into and out of each fill.
*   **Musicality**: While the fills are short, they should be played with musical intent. Consider the dynamics and articulation to match the overall mood of the coda.
*   **Precision**: One-measure fills require precise timing to fit perfectly within the song's structure. Use a [[Metronome]] to ensure accuracy.
