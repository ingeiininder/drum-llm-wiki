# Third Ending Fill (Unit 9)
The fill designated for the [[B_Section_Endings_Unit_9|third ending]] of the B section is designed to be played with alternating [[Single Strokes]].

**Execution:**
*   The fill should be executed using a continuous stream of alternating single strokes (R L R L R L...).
*   Focus on evenness in dynamics and spacing between each stroke.
*   The alternating sticking ensures fluidity and speed, making it a versatile choice for a concluding fill before moving to the next section.

**Pro-Tip**: While the basic instruction is alternating single strokes, consider the context of the song. This fill likely serves as a transition or a build-up. Ensure your dynamics match the energy of the piece, potentially starting softer and building, or maintaining a consistent, driving volume. Practice with a [[Metronome]] to ensure rhythmic accuracy and evenness.
