# D.C. al Fine
A common musical instruction indicating how to navigate repeats and endings in a piece:
*   **D.C.** (da capo): Italian for "from the beginning," instructing the performer to return to the start of the chart.
*   **Al Fine**: Italian for "to the end," instructing the performer to stop playing once they reach the section marked "Fine."

Together, D.C. al Fine means to return to the beginning of the chart and play through until the "Fine" mark. If the instruction includes "repeats are good," it indicates that [[First and Second Endings]] should be observed during the repeated section.
