# Bad Country (Track)

"Bad Country" is a high-energy, uptempo performance track designed to develop the unrelenting feel required for [[Funk-Rock_Drumming]]. The track serves as a dual-purpose exercise for both [[Double_Bass_Drumming]] and single-foot endurance.

### Performance Characteristics
- **[[Tempo|Tempo]]**: Significantly faster than standard curriculum tracks, requiring high-velocity control.
- **Feel**: High-energy punk-[[Rock|rock]].
- **Technical Focus**: The [[Groove|groove]] features a challenging "a" of [[Beat|beat]] 3 played with the left foot (in a double-kick context), which is frequently missed by developing players.

### Educational Objectives
1. **Left Foot Isolation**: The curriculum recommends playing the entire track using only the left foot to address balance issues and strengthen the "dreaded left side."
2. **Rhythmic Accuracy**: Mastering the quick doubles in the [[Bass_Drum|bass drum]] while maintaining a consistent [[Back-Beat]].
3. **Feel Switching**: Practicing the transition between [[Half-Time_Feel]] and regular time to understand how different subdivisions alter the musical "weight."

**Pro-Tip**: When playing at these speeds, focus on a relaxed [[Ankle_Motion]] technique. Tension in the shins is a common pitfall that will lead to early fatigue and "[[Burying_the_Beater|burying the beater]]."
