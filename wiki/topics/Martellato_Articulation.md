# [[Martellato|Martellato]] Articulation

**[[Martellato|Martellato]]** (Italian for "hammered") is an articulation mark indicating that a note should be played with significant force and a sharp, sudden attack.

### Application in Drumming
In a drum [[Chart|chart]], [[Martellato|martellato]] [[Eighth_Notes|eighth notes]] (often indicated by a heavy accent or wedge symbol) require:
*   A strong, downward stroke.
*   Immediate muffling or a "[[Staccato|staccato]]" feel, depending on the context.
*   High velocity to match the "hammered" description.

### Context
In [[Drum_Reading]], [[Martellato|martellato]] [[Articulations|articulations]] are used to end phrases (such as the end of a 10-bar phrase) to provide a definitive, aggressive conclusion to the section.
