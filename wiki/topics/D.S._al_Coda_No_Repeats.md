# D.S. al Coda (No Repeats)
The directive "D.S. al Coda (no repeats)" or "D.S. al Coda (take 2nd ending)" is a specific instruction for navigating a [[D.S._al_Coda]] when [[First_Ending]]s and [[Second_Ending]]s are present.

*   **Meaning**: When you encounter the "D.S. al Coda" directive, you return to the "segno" sign. On this return, you are instructed to *skip* any [[Repeat_Signs|repeat signs]] and [[First_Ending]]s, proceeding directly to the [[Second_Ending]].
*   **Execution**:
    1.  Play through the music until you reach the "D.S. al Coda" instruction.
    2.  Go back to the "segno" sign.
    3.  Play the section again. When you reach the point where a repeat would normally occur, *skip* the [[First_Ending]] entirely.
    4.  Proceed directly to the [[Second_Ending]].
    5.  Continue playing until you reach the "To Coda" sign, then jump to the Coda section.

**Pro-Tip**: This instruction streamlines the form, often shortening the piece or a section. It's vital to clearly understand this directive to avoid playing extra measures or missing the intended transition. Always confirm with the bandleader if the instruction is unclear.
