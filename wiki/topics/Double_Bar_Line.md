# Double [[Bar_Line|Bar Line]]
A double [[Bar_Line|bar line]] is a type of [[Bar_Lines]] used in [[Music_Notation]] to visually separate distinct sections of music within a single piece. It consists of two parallel vertical lines.

## Function
*   **Section Separation**: The primary purpose of a double [[Bar_Line|bar line]] is to indicate a change in musical form or structure. This could signify:
    *   A change in [[Tempo]].
    *   A change in [[Time_Signature]].
    *   A change in [[Key_Signature]].
    *   The end of a major section (e.g., verse, chorus, bridge).
*   **Visual Aid**: It serves as a clear visual cue for the performer, helping them to keep their place and anticipate upcoming changes or new musical ideas while reading the score.

Unlike a single [[Bar_Line|bar line]] which simply divides [[Measure_Music_Notation|measures]], the double [[Bar_Line|bar line]] implies a more significant structural division.
