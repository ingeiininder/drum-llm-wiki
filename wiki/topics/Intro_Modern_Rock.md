# Intro (Modern Rock)
The `[[Intro_Modern_Rock]]` for this tune is a four-bar `[[Guitar]]` part designed to establish the `[[Feel]]` of the piece. As the drummer, your role during this introduction includes providing a `[[Count-In]]` for the guitar player.

The recommended count-in is a two-bar count-in, often vocalized as "One," "Two," "Three" for each beat in `[[3_4_Time_Signature]]`, as shown in `[[Ex 9.2]]`.

**Pro-Tip:** A clear and confident `[[Count-In]]` is essential for a strong start to any song. Practice your count-in to be precise and in time with the intended `[[Tempo]]`. Even though the guitar plays alone, your count-in sets the stage for the entire band. Pay attention to the `[[Dynamics]]` and `[[Articulations]]` of the guitar intro to understand the overall mood and `[[Groove]]` you'll be entering.
