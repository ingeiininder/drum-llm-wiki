# Final [[Bar_Line|Bar Line]]
The final [[Bar_Line|bar line]] is a specific type of [[Bar_Lines]] used in [[Music_Notation]] to unequivocally mark the conclusion of a piece of music. It is composed of two parallel vertical lines, with the second line being noticeably thicker than the first.

## Function
*   **Indicates Ending**: Its sole purpose is to confirm the absolute end of a musical composition.
*   **Visual Clarity**: The distinct appearance of the final [[Bar_Line|bar line]] provides a clear visual signal to the performer that no further music is to be played.

It is the definitive symbol for the completion of a musical work, distinguishing it from [[Double_Bar_Line]]s which merely separate sections within a piece.
