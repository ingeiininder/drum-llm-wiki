# Funk

Funk is a rhythm-centric genre that rose to prominence in the mid-1960s and 1970s. For drummers, it represents one of the most physically demanding and creatively rewarding styles, emphasizing precision, syncopation, and a "deep" pocket.

## Historical Foundations

- **The Golden Era**: Funk drumming was defined between 1965 and 1975 by pioneering artists like **James Brown**, **Sly and the Family Stone**, and **Tower of Power**.
- **The Funk Brothers**: A group of studio musicians in Detroit who helped define the Motown and Funk sound, specifically drummers like **Benny Benjamin**, **Richard "Pistol" Allen**, and **Uriel Jones**.
- **New Orleans Influence**: The "Second Line" tradition of New Orleans deeply influenced the "loose and dirty" [[Hi-Hat|hi-hat]] feel characteristic of authentic funk recordings.

## Technical Characteristics

- **Layered Rhythms**: Funk involves layering highly syncopated [[Bass_Drum|bass drum]] patterns underneath consistent but detailed [[Hi-Hat|hi-hat]] and snare patterns.
- **Articulation and Sound**:
    - **[[Snare_Drum|Snare Drum]]**: "Light Rim Shots" are preferred to make the drum "speak" and add character without excessive volume.
    - **[[Hi-Hat|Hi-Hat]]**: Often played slightly loose or "dirty" to capture the vibe of classic soul and funk recordings.
    - **Backbeat**: A strong, definitive, and unwavering backbeat on 2 and 4 is the anchor of the soul-funk style.
- **Subdivisions**: The **Sixteenth Note** is the essential rhythmic value for funk, fusion, and related styles.

## Sub-Styles and Variations

- **Soul-Funk**: Places equal emphasis on catchy songwriting, [[Jazz|jazz]] improvisation, and swinging grooves. (e.g., **Stevie Wonder**, **The Roots**).
- **Soul-[[Jazz|Jazz]]**: A hybrid coming from a [[Jazz|jazz]] perspective but focusing on repetitive, syncopated funk grooves.
- **Odd-Time Funk**: Contemporary explorers (like **Bill Stewart**) apply funk [[Phrasing|phrasing]] to odd meters like 5/4 and 7/4.

## Influential Masters
- **Clyde Stubblefield** and **Jabo Starks** (James Brown)
- **Garibaldi** (Tower of Power)
- **Idris Muhammad** and **Bill Stewart** ([[Jazz|Jazz]]-Funk)
- **Steve Jordan** (Modern Funk-[[Rock|Rock]])
