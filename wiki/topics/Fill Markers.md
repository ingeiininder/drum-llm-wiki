# Fill Markers
Notation used to indicate transition fills within a drum chart. Similar to how drum set grooves are marked with time slashes, fill markers can specify the length of the fill (e.g., 1-beat, 2-beat, 3-beat, 4-beat). In the Unit 3 chart, fill markers are strategically placed in three locations: the second ending of Letter A, bar 8 of letter B, and the last bar of the A section at the end of the tune (just before the [[D.C. al Fine]] instruction).
