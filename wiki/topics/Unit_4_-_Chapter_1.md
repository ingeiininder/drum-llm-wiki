# Unit 4 - Chapter 1
This chapter, titled "Intro to `[[Two-Handed 16ths]]`," delves into the application of `[[16th Notes]]` using both hands, a cornerstone of modern `[[Rock Music Styles]]` and `[[Metal]]`. It explores how this technique, often fused with elements of `[[Synth-Pop]]` and `[[Heavy Metal]]`, creates dynamic and `[[Syncopated Beats]]` within an `[[Alternative Rock]]` and metal context. The chapter also introduces the `[[ABAB 32-Bar Form]]` as a structural framework for these grooves.
