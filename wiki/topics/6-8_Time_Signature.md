# 6/8 Time Signature

The **6/8 Time Signature** is a compound meter where there are six beats per measure, and the **eighth note** receives one beat.

### Note Values in 6/8
In 6/8, notes are typically grouped into two clusters of three eighth notes.
- **Half Note**: 6 beats (one per measure).
- **Dotted Quarter Note**: 3 beats (two per measure).
- **Quarter Note**: 2 beats (three per measure).
- **Eighth Note**: 1 beat (six per measure).

### Counting Strategies
The method of counting 6/8 depends entirely on the tempo:
1. **Slow Tempo**: Count every eighth note (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6). Tap your foot on every beat.
2. **Fast Tempo**: Feel the meter in "two." The strong pulses land on beats 1 and 4. Tap your foot only on 1 and 4. This creates a "dotted quarter note" pulse.

### Common Rests
- **Dotted Quarter Rest**: Covers half a measure (3 beats).
- **Quarter Rest**: Covers 2 beats.
- **Eighth Rest**: Covers 1 beat.
