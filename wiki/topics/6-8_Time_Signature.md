# 6/8 [[Time_Signature|Time Signature]]

The **6/8 [[Time_Signature|Time Signature]]** is a compound [[Meter|meter]] where there are six beats per [[Measure|measure]], and the **eighth note** receives one [[Beat|beat]].

### Note Values in 6/8
In 6/8, notes are typically grouped into two clusters of three [[Eighth_Notes|eighth notes]].
- **Half Note**: 6 beats (one per [[Measure|measure]]).
- **[[Dotted_Quarter_Note|Dotted Quarter Note]]**: 3 beats (two per [[Measure|measure]]).
- **Quarter Note**: 2 beats (three per [[Measure|measure]]).
- **Eighth Note**: 1 [[Beat|beat]] (six per [[Measure|measure]]).

### Counting Strategies
The method of counting 6/8 depends entirely on the [[Tempo|tempo]]:
1. **Slow [[Tempo|Tempo]]**: Count every eighth note (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6). Tap your foot on every [[Beat|beat]].
2. **Fast [[Tempo|Tempo]]**: Feel the [[Meter|meter]] in "two." The strong pulses land on beats 1 and 4. Tap your foot only on 1 and 4. This creates a "[[Dotted_Quarter_Note|dotted quarter note]]" [[Pulse|pulse]].

### Common [[Rests|Rests]]
- **Dotted Quarter Rest**: Covers half a [[Measure|measure]] (3 beats).
- **Quarter Rest**: Covers 2 beats.
- **Eighth Rest**: Covers 1 [[Beat|beat]].
