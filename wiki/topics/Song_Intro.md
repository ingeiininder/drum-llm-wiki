# Song Intro
The "Intro" of a tune is typically a short musical phrase, usually four to eight bars in length, designed to establish the feel, tempo, and overall vibe of the song before the main [[Verse_Section_Letter_A]] or vocals begin. In the context of [[Country-Pop Genre]], an intro might feature a [[Pre-Programmed Drum Loop]] or a distinctive instrumental lick.

For [[Ex 5.1 Country Pop Intro]], the intro is four bars long, featuring a drum loop with a guitar lick entering on bars 3-4. The instructor may manage the loop initially, with students taking responsibility in later units.

**Pro-Tip:** The intro sets the stage. As a drummer, your role is to establish the [[Groove]] and energy without giving everything away too soon. If using a loop, ensure your acoustic playing locks in perfectly with it. If playing a standalone intro, make it compelling and indicative of the song's character.
