# Multiple-Bar Phrases (Drumming)
This concept refers to drum patterns, particularly [[Bass_Drum|bass drum]] patterns, that extend over several measures before repeating, rather than repeating every single bar. For example, the [[A_Section_Groove_(Unit_3)]] features a [[Four-Bar_Bass_Drum_Pattern|four-bar bass drum pattern]], while the [[B_Section_Groove_(Unit_3)]] has a [[Two-Bar_Bass_Drum_Pattern|two-bar bass drum pattern]]. This technique adds complexity and variation to the rhythmic structure of a piece.
