# Unit 4 Homework
This homework assignment focuses on reinforcing the concepts and techniques learned in Unit 4, particularly concerning [[Drum Rudiment]]s (specifically ruffs) and [[Hi-Hat Technique]], while also reviewing previous material.

**Tasks:**
1.  **Explain Rudiments:** Be able to explain what [[Drum Rudiment]]s are and why they are beneficial for drummers.
2.  **Play Ruff Rudiments:** Be able to play all of the [[Ruff Rudiments]] (e.g., [[Five-Stroke Ruff]], [[Seven-Stroke Ruff]], [[Three-Stroke Ruff]]) in their various forms (standard, inverted, accented variations).
3.  **Master Studies Exercises:** Be able to play exercises 13-24 on pages 8 and 9 of [[Master Studies]].
4.  **Bass Drum Control Review:** Review the [[Bass Drum Control Exercises]] from [[Unit 3 Bass Drum Technique]].
5.  **Midterm Preparation:** Go over the midterm prep materials to check your strengths and weaknesses.

**Pro-Tip:**
Don't just aim for mechanical execution. When explaining rudiments, articulate their musical applications and how they contribute to overall [[Drum Technique]]. For playing exercises, focus on musicality, dynamics, and consistency. The review sections are crucial for building a strong foundation and identifying areas that need more attention before moving forward.
