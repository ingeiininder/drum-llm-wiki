# D.C. al Fine
[[D.C. al Fine]] is an Italian musical direction that stands for "Da Capo al Fine," which translates to "from the beginning to the Fine." It instructs the performer to "go back to the beginning of the chart and end at Fine." The term 'Fine' (pronounced FEE-nay) is a separate marking that indicates the actual ending point of the piece after the repeat.

**Pro-Tip:** Always be aware of [[D.C. al Fine]] and other navigation markings (like D.S. al Coda, etc.) in charts. Missing these can lead to significant performance errors, causing the band to get lost or end abruptly. Scan your charts for these directions before playing.
