# Accented Hi-Hat Note Technique
To achieve a clear, loud accented sound on the [[hi-hat]]:
1.  **Stick Placement:** Use the shoulder of the [[drum stick]].
2.  **Contact Point:** The contact point on the stick should be approximately a third of the way down from the tip.
3.  **Motion:** The primary motion for playing the loud, accented note should come from your [[Wrist]].
