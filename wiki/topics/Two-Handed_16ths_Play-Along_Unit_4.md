# [[Two-Handed_16ths|Two-Handed 16ths]] Play-Along Unit 4
This unit focuses on developing proficiency with [[Two-Handed_16th_Notes]] in a [[Play-Along]] context, specifically within the [[Hard_Rock]] genre. Students will engage with exercises like [[Ex_4.18_Hard_Rock_Groove]] that require precise control over [[Hi-Hat_Sound_Production]] and the integration of [[Drum_Fill]]s.

**Pro-Tip:** Mastering two-handed [[16th_Notes|16th notes]] on the [[Hi-Hat|hi-hat]] is fundamental for many contemporary drumming styles. Pay close attention to consistency in [[Dynamics|dynamics]] and timing between both hands. Even if one hand is dominant, strive for equal articulation and power from the non-dominant hand.
