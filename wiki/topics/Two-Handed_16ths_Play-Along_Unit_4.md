# Two-Handed 16ths Play-Along Unit 4
This unit focuses on developing proficiency with [[Two-Handed 16th Notes]] in a [[Play-Along]] context, specifically within the [[Hard Rock]] genre. Students will engage with exercises like [[Ex 4.18 Hard Rock Groove]] that require precise control over [[Hi-Hat Sound Production]] and the integration of [[Drum Fill]]s.

**Pro-Tip:** Mastering two-handed 16th notes on the hi-hat is fundamental for many contemporary drumming styles. Pay close attention to consistency in dynamics and timing between both hands. Even if one hand is dominant, strive for equal articulation and power from the non-dominant hand.
