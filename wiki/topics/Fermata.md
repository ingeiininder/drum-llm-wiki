# Fermata
A [[Fermata]] (also known as an extended ending, hold, or pause) is an element of musical notation indicating that a note should be sustained for longer than its written note value would typically indicate.

The exact duration for which the note is held is at the discretion of the drummer. It is also the drummer's responsibility to "cut-off" the band and conclude this extended hold.
