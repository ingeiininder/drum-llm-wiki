# Triplet [[Fill|Fill]]-Ins
Triplet [[Fill|fill]]-ins are used to maintain momentum and "[[Time_Feel|time feel]]" between [[Ensemble_Figures]] in a swung or [[Jazz|jazz]] context.

### Technical Approaches
1.  **Right-Hand Lead**: The right hand stays on the [[Ride_Cymbal|ride cymbal]] (often "shouldering" the edge) while the left hand fills in the remaining [[Triplet_Partials|triplet partials]] on the snare. This is highly effective in **[[Jazz|Jazz]] Combo** settings.
2.  **Hand-to-Hand (Alternating)**: Continuous `R L R L R L` [[Triplets|triplets]]. This provides a denser, more driving sound suitable for **[[Rock_Shuffle|Rock Shuffle]]** or **[[Blues|Blues]]** settings.

### Practice Rotation
To master these, practice a figure using three distinct passes:
*   **Pass 1**: Alternating [[Triplets|triplets]] on the snare, accenting the figure.
*   **Pass 2**: Alternating [[Triplets|triplets]] on the snare, playing the figure on crashes with [[Bass_Drum|bass drum]].
*   **Pass 3**: Right-hand lead technique on the [[Ride_Cymbal|ride cymbal]].

**Pro-Tip**: Use these fills to "bridge the gap" between two-bar phrases, ensuring the energy doesn't drop during the [[Rests|rests]].
