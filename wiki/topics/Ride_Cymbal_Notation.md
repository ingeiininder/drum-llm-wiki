# Ride Cymbal (Notation)
In [[Drum_Set_Notation_Key]], the [[Ride_Cymbal]] is notated with a distinct symbol to differentiate it from drums and other cymbals.

## Standard Placement
*   The ride cymbal is notated with an **'X' [[Note_Head]]**.
*   Its typical position is on the **top line** of [[The_Staff]] or sometimes on a ledger line directly above the staff.

## Function in Notation
Ride cymbal notation indicates when the ride cymbal should be played, often providing a continuous rhythmic pattern (e.g., a [[Swing]] pattern) that drives the [[Groove|groove]]. Specific markings may indicate playing on the bell of the cymbal or the edge.
