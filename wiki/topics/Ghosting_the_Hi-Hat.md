# Ghosting the Hi-Hat

**Ghosting the Hi-Hat** involves playing unaccented notes at a *pianissimo* volume between the primary accents of a groove. This technique adds "air" and depth to the rhythm without overpowering the lead instruments.

### Implementation
1. **Isolate Accents**: First, practice the groove playing only the accented notes (usually the downbeats or specific syncopations).
2. **Add Ghost Notes**: Lightly fill in the remaining 16th-note subdivisions.
3. **Dynamic Contrast**: The gap between the accent and the ghost note must be significant to achieve the "Modern Rock" or "Funk" sound.
