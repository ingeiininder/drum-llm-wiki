# Ghosting the [[Hi-Hat|Hi-Hat]]

**Ghosting the [[Hi-Hat|Hi-Hat]]** involves playing unaccented notes at a *pianissimo* volume between the primary [[Accents|accents]] of a [[Groove|groove]]. This technique adds "air" and depth to the rhythm without overpowering the lead instruments.

### Implementation
1. **Isolate [[Accents|Accents]]**: First, practice the [[Groove|groove]] playing only the accented notes (usually the downbeats or specific syncopations).
2. **Add [[Ghost_Notes|Ghost Notes]]**: Lightly [[Fill|fill]] in the remaining 16th-note subdivisions.
3. **Dynamic Contrast**: The gap between the accent and the ghost note must be significant to achieve the "Modern [[Rock|Rock]]" or "[[Funk|Funk]]" sound.
