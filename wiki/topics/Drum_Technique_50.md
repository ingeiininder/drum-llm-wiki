# Drum Technique 50
[[Drum Technique 50]] is a foundational unit or course within the Musicians Institute percussion curriculum, focusing on developing core drumming techniques. This module covers essential skills such as [[Drum Rolls]] and their [[Roll Variations]], advanced [[Coordination]] exercises like the [[Bass Drum and Hi-Hat Coordination Exercise]], and the application of studies from resources like the [[Master_Studies_Book]]. The curriculum emphasizes structured practice, including routines like [[Focused Roll Practice]] and the [[Master Studies Daily Practice Routine]], alongside specific techniques such as [[Mirror Practice for Stick Heights]] and [[Counting Out Loud]].

**Pro-Tip**: Treat each lesson within this unit as a building block. Solidify the fundamentals before moving to more advanced concepts.
**Common Pitfall**: Rushing through the curriculum without fully mastering each technique, leading to gaps in foundational skills.
