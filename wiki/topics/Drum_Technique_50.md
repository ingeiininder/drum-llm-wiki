# Drum Technique 50
[[Drum_Technique_50]] is a foundational unit or course within the Musicians Institute percussion curriculum, focusing on developing core drumming techniques. This module covers essential skills such as [[Drum_Rolls]] and their [[Roll_Variations]], advanced [[Coordination]] exercises like the [[Bass_Drum_and_Hi-Hat_Coordination_Exercise]], and the application of studies from resources like the [[Master_Studies_Book]]. The curriculum emphasizes structured practice, including routines like [[Focused_Roll_Practice]] and the [[Master_Studies_Daily_Practice_Routine]], alongside specific techniques such as [[Mirror_Practice_for_Stick_Heights]] and [[Counting_Out_Loud]].

**Pro-Tip**: Treat each lesson within this unit as a building block. Solidify the fundamentals before moving to more advanced concepts.
**Common Pitfall**: Rushing through the curriculum without fully mastering each technique, leading to gaps in foundational skills.
