# Rudimental Drumming
[[Rudimental_Drumming]] refers to the practice and study of drum [[Rudiments|rudiments]], which are fundamental patterns and exercises that form the building blocks of drumming technique. It is introduced as a key topic in Unit 4 of "[[Drum_Technique_1_(MI_Curriculum_Series)]]".

**What are [[Rudiments|Rudiments]]?**
[[Rudiments|Rudiments]] are standardized [[Sticking_Patterns|sticking patterns]] and rhythmic figures, typically played on the [[Snare_Drum|snare drum]], that develop [[Stick_Control]], coordination, speed, and endurance. They are analogous to scales and arpeggios for melodic instruments.

**Key Categories of [[Rudiments|Rudiments]] (often based on the Percussive Arts Society International Drum [[Rudiments|Rudiments]]):**
*   **Single Stroke Roll:** [[Single_Stroke_Roll]]
*   **Multiple Bounce Roll:** [[Multiple_Bounce_Roll]]
*   **Double Stroke Roll:** [[Double_Stroke_Roll]] (e.g., [[Five_Stroke_Roll]], [[Six_Stroke_Roll]], [[Seven-Stroke_Roll]], [[Ten-Stroke_Roll]])
*   **Diddles:** (e.g., [[Single_Paradiddle]], [[Double_Paradiddle]], [[Triple_Paradiddle]], [[Paradiddle-diddle]])
*   **Flam [[Rudiments|Rudiments]]:** (e.g., [[Flams]], [[Flam_Accent]], [[Flam_Paradiddle]])
*   **Drag [[Rudiments|Rudiments]]:** (e.g., [[Drag]], [[Three-Stroke_Ruff]], [[Four-Stroke_Ruff]], [[Five-Stroke_Ruff]], [[Seven-Stroke_Ruff]])

**Benefits of Rudimental Drumming:**
*   **Technical Proficiency:** Develops precision, consistency, and control in both hands.
*   **Speed and Endurance:** Builds the physical capabilities required for faster and longer playing.
*   **Coordination:** Improves the ability to execute complex patterns smoothly.
*   **Musical Vocabulary:** Provides a foundation of rhythmic patterns that can be applied creatively across the drum kit and in various musical styles.

**Pro-Tip:** Don't view [[Rudiments|rudiments]] as mere exercises; they are musical phrases. Practice them slowly and deliberately, focusing on evenness, [[Dynamics|dynamics]], and a relaxed approach. Once mastered on the [[Snare_Drum|snare drum]], explore applying them around the [[Drum_Kit]] to create grooves and fills. Regular "[[Rudimental_Recall]]" practice is essential for long-term retention and development.
