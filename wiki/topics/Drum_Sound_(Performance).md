# Drum Sound (Performance)
Just as a musical [[Chart|chart]] has a particular [[Groove|groove]], phrase length, and chord progression, it also implies a particular drum sound. Achieving the correct [[Drum_Sound]] is a crucial aspect of performance.

The [[Bass_Drum]] and [[Snare_Drum]] sounds established in the previous unit should carry over and be applied to the current [[Chart|chart]]. This continuity helps maintain a consistent and appropriate sonic character throughout the performance.
