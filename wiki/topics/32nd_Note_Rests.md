# 32nd Note [[Rests|Rests]]

The **32nd rest** is the silent counterpart to the [[32nd_Note_Notation]]. It is notated with a vertical stem and **three small arms** (or flags) extending to the left.

### Rhythmic Equivalencies
*   **Two 32nd [[Rests|rests]]** = One [[16th_Note]] rest.
*   **Four 32nd [[Rests|rests]]** = One [[Eighth_Note]] rest.

### Common Pitfall
In dense charts, 32nd [[Rests|rests]] can be easily overlooked or confused with 16th [[Rests|rests]]. Always check the number of "arms" on the rest symbol to confirm the required [[Duration|duration]] of silence.
