# 32nd Note Rests

The **32nd rest** is the silent counterpart to the [[32nd Note Notation]]. It is notated with a vertical stem and **three small arms** (or flags) extending to the left.

### Rhythmic Equivalencies
*   **Two 32nd rests** = One [[16th Note]] rest.
*   **Four 32nd rests** = One [[Eighth Note]] rest.

### Common Pitfall
In dense charts, 32nd rests can be easily overlooked or confused with 16th rests. Always check the number of "arms" on the rest symbol to confirm the required duration of silence.
