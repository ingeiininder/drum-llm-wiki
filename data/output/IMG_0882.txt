Whole Notes and Half Notes Whole note and half notes should be treated in a similar manner as quarter notes (with the target beat being the full downbeat before the figure): However; in this case the set-up fill should also match the length of the ensemble figure Thus, a half note (worth two beats) , should be preceded by a two-beat fill
