The Ending: Slight Ritardando The second-to-last bar of the chart indicates to slightly slow the tempo down and ease into the final bar (fermata) This is indicated with the words "slight rit" It is important to make eye contact with the other band members so you are all slowing down at the same pace. It is your job to lead this gradual slowdown, so make sure you are clear and confident when executing this ritardando. Ex 3.16
