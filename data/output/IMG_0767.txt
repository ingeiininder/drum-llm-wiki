Comping Part 2: Duration Long Notes In the last unit's jazz example; the drums comped nearly all of the written melody: In this chart; the drums support the melody line by accompanying and accentuating the long notes within each phrase. In this context, the eighth notes and quarter notes would be considered short notes: Any half notes or tied notes (where the tie extends the duration of the note) would be considered a long note.
