Letter B: Groove and Sound At letter B, the playing surface switches to the ride cymbal The ride cymbal pattern is the same as the hi- hat pattern (continuous swung I6ths) and must be played with clear sound, avoiding too much "washiness:" Just like letter A,the groove at letter B is a two-bar pattern with significant syncopation present in the snare drum and bass drum parts However; beat 1 ofthe A and B patterns are different: In letter A the bass drum plays on "1 & "where in letter B the bass drum plays"1 ah"on beat 1.
