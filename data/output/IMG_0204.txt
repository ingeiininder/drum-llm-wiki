Chapter 2: Performance Notes and Sound The A section features a two-bar groove with a syncopated bass drum and a snare hit on beat 1 of every other bar The hi-hat should be closed and played consistently without any accented notes. The bass drum should be played strong; but with the beater off the head. The snare drum should be a warm, center snare hit (not a rimshot) The fill in the third ending should be played with alternating single strokes For example: Ex 9.7
