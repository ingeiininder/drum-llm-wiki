Pickup Notes and Count In The tune starts with a two-beat pickup phrase played by the guitar and bass The drums also play a strong downbeat on beat 3,which should be short and deliberate: One way to orchestrate this figure is to play the hi-hat and snare drum together: For example: Ex 5.7
In order to efficiently count in the band, please confidently (and forcefully) count beats and 2 like this: Ex 5.8
Count: "One' 'Two"
Fills and Figures The first fill (bar 8 leading into A) mimics a recurring rhythmic figure played by the guitar and bass. Since this fill is written out in detail, you will need to play it without alteration. The appropriate sticking is notated below: Ex 5.9
9 LL R
