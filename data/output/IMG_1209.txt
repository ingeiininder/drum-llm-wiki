Drumset Interpretation Part II Remember; lead sheets do not contain 'beats" The drummer must create the appropriate feel (and corresponding sound) based on the style indicated (ballad, Latin, shuffle etc) Keep in mind that you must also be selective when choosing which figures to play within the lead sheet: Fills and fill markers are not written in either;and, since there are multiple sections and grooves, it is up to you to set up the appropriate transition for each new section.
Lastly, it is important to note that you must still observe the principles that we have discussed in the last three books: time; feel, note durations, and marking the form:.
