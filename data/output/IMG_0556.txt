Homework Be able to play all the rolls and their respective variations: Be able to play exercises 13-24 on pages 14 and 15 of Master Studies: Be able to play the bass drum & hi-hat coordination exercise without holding on to anything for balance:
Practice Suggestions Read the entire lesson at least twice: Use a mirror to help monitor your stick heights as you work through the roll exercises: Count; out loud, the respective counting system for each roll variation. Pick two roll rudiments and play all oftheir respective variations for two minutes each: Alternate each day which two rolls you will focus on: Every day, play four of the twelve Master Studies exercises for two minutes each: Choose different set of four each day: As you play down the bass drum & hi-hat coordination exercises focus on your balance resting on your seat; which will allow you to produce a "chippy" hi-hat sound and authoritative bass drum performance Use four-part counting as you play through the bass drum syncopation exercise.
