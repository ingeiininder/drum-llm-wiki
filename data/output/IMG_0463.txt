Quarter note is worth one beat
2/4 is commonly used for songs with a meter (Unit 1) of two. Country train beats, polkas and marches are often written in 2/4. The frequent emphasis placed on beat is the main difference between 2/4 and 4/4 (other than that 2/4 contains two beats less than 4/4). For instance; in 4/4, beat of every measure is considered the strong beat (i.e:: 1,2,3,4,1,2,3,4):
Whereas in 2/4,beat is also considered a strong beat; but it occurs twice as often (i.e: - 1,2,1,2,1,2).
