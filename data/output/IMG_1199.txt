The Intro The intro is comprised of two two-bar phrases (for a total of four bars) For the first (and third) bar; the entire band plays the figures together in unison. During the second (and fourth) bar the drums play the figures alone; which is stated by the text "drums only" For example: Ex 1.2 Intro Bb7("9) Bb7(*9)
