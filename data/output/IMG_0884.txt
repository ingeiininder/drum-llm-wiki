Ensemble Figures Occurring on Upbeats When upbeats are played,the musical momentum naturally increases and most musicians tend to push the tempo ahead of the beat: Therefore; a strong downbeat should be provided (by the drummer) prior to executing an upbeat ensemble figure When the band "feels" where the drummer places the downbeat; they will react together as a unified group; tightly executing the figure
