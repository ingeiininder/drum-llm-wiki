Letter € Letter € is a ten-bar phrase with many ensemble figures and articulation markings. After playing two bars of time; you must set up the first group of ensemble figures Each of the figures in this first phrase has a length of one and a half beats, spanning across bars 3 and 4. There is room in between each figure for two eighth notes Be sure to experiment with different combinations of eighth notes between the snare drum, toms and bass drum Ex 10.9 Each fiqure is worth one and a half beats.
In bars 5 and 6 there are more ensemble figures: Again, each figure has a duration lasting one and a half beats. In bar 7 there are four quarter notes marked staccato. These should be played as short as possible leading into bar 8. At bar 8 there is a break on beat 1, exaggerated with a strong quarter note (marked as martellato) on beat 1. On beat 4 of bar 8 there is an ensemble figure tied to a two-bar hold that you can fill through: Be sure to notice the decrescendo that occurs over bars 9 and 10.
