Ensemble Figure: A Proper Count-In Todays chart begins with a one-and-a-half beat ensemble figure pickup on the "&" of beat 3. This occurs before the start of the chart and the 12-bar form: Due to the slow tempo (and in order for the whole band to play this correctly), it is imperative that you give a three-beat count directly into beat 3,and then hit the above ensemble figure with the band.
