One big reason why you must have endurance is simply So you can stay relaxed while playing grooves Many young players do not realize the stamina (and control) required to maintain even the simplest of grooves. Also, while you may be able to get through a tune with plenty of gas left in the tank, what about 90-minute set going from song to song without a chance to rest? Over the course of a gig, you must have the endurance to pace yourself and maintain your stamina. Young drummers often break from the groove to play a fill merely because they: are struggling to physically maintain a groove. The following exercises will help you develop control and stamina by playing a repeated pattern (while adding some varying nuances).
