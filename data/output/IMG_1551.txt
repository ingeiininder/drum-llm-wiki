Oftentimes in the recording studio charts are not available As such, in this unit you are presented with a medium Latin tune (mambo) in which you will need to create your own chart:
Listen to the track twice: During the first listen pay close attention to the feel; overall form, section lengths and ensemble figures. It is essential to make sure you decipher the clave patternl During the second listen you will be able to double-check your initial notes and grab more specific details such as dynamics, articulation Be sure to earmark where you will change grooves; switch playing surfaces and play set-up fills
