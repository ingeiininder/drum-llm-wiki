Chapter 2: Drumset Mobility: Flams Flams are one of the most effective rudiments to apply to the drumset when a specific note needs to be enhanced and/or featured. By utilizing a few of the previously-studied flam rudiments and applying them to various voicings, you will quickly understand the virtually unlimited possibilities available to you:
All of the following exercises with a flam being split between a tom and snare will always be played in that order: tom, snare. Playing the snare before the tom is an option but will not articulate the relationship ofthe flam as clearly. Also, accents and grace notes are subjective within the following ideas  As you move around the kit some accents will feel natural for you to play, others will not The main objective is to focus on the rhythmic relationship between the two notes ofthe flam,not the dynamic relationship Therefore,no accents will be written:
