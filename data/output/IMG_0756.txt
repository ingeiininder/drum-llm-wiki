Chapter 2: Performance Notes and Sound The Jazz Balance of Sound In previous lessons, we have focused on a pop-rock balance of sound This places the main emphasis (and strong dynamic) on the kick and snare; while having the hi-hat placed quite below these within our "players mix" However; in jazz this balance is reversed. It features the ride cymbal as the main (loudest) voice; thehi- hat a close second, with the snare just below the hi-hat and the bass drum as the softest: For example: Ex 6.4
