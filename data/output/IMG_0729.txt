Bass Drum The bass drum pattern should be played with conviction, with an open "off-the-head" articulation and a dynamic of mezzo piano (medium soft). This will be accomplished by using the felt side (or a soft-surfaced) bass drum beater; which will give the drum a round, open tone and help blend the bass drum's sound with the snare drum cross-stick dynamic

Letter € Letter € (the chorus) features another similar 16th-note based 6/8 groove; which is also preformed as an eight-bar phrase. All eight bars are played utilizing the same open; "off-the-head" bass drum articulation as letters A and B. However; this groove is performed on the ride cymbal, and the hi-hat foot closes on beats 2,3, 5 and 6.
