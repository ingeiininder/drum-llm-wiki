MEDIUM SWING written by Steve Smith, Adam Nussbaum, Jay Anderson excerpted from the DVD The Art of Playing with Brushes used by permission from the author and Hudson Music LLC 02007 Hudson Music; LLC
Oftentimes in the recording studio charts are not available. As such, in this unit you are presented with a medium swing tune in which you will need to create your own chart: Listen to the track twice
During the first listen pay close attention to the feel, overall form, section lengths and ensemble figures During the second listen you will be able to double-check your initial notes and grab more specific details such as dynamics, articulation Be sure to earmark where you will change grooves, switch playing surfaces and play set-up fills
Note: your one detailed performance note from the producer is to use brushes for the entire tune:
