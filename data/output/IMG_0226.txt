Time Signature A time signature indicates the meter of a piece of music and the corresponding note value used within its notation. Placed to the right of the clef,the time signature is displayed as a stack of two numbers The top number indicates how many beats there are in each measure. The bottom number displays the value of each beat: (Values will be explained in the next chapter) For example: Ex 1.12a
