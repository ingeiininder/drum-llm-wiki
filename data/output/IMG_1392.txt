Chapter 2: Fill Development Many students often feel as though they do not have a large arsenal of fills at their disposal Students go from teacher to teacher looking for that magic fill to help them get to "the next level" What these students are lacking is creativity: As sensitive musicians; we always want to impress an audience or the other band members when playing (especially when soloing) There is a fear among young musicians that people listening will find that performers dont have a deep vocabulary ifthey repeat themselves This is not the case: Ifyou play something someone really likes, they probably want to hear more of it The trick is to repackage your ideas Think of a fill idea as a seed You play the initial idea and then develop the idea until it transforms into something new:
