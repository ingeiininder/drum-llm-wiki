Unit Ne reading | techNIque pertOrMaNce Chapter 1: Intro to Modern Rock It is important for all drummers to understand how to play and read in the 3/4 time signature: 3/4 is very common and is used by many bands and artists in practically all genres of music: Most recently; popular bands such as Paramore; Muse and Radiohead have recorded many great tunes in 3/4. Technically; from a drummer's perspective, 3/4is really no different than any other style or feel. One trap you can fall into is to allow the tempo to rush: The downbeat of in every measure is very strong in 3/4 time, and it is common for young players to undervalue to importance of beats 2 and 3. Practicing 3/4 time: Use a metronomel A great way to develop your 3/4 time feel is to use a metronome: Let's look at two simple methods:
Method 1: Quarter-Note Click Set your metronome to 100 BPM with no accented clicks. Treating each click with a value of a quarter note, play the following groove: Ex 9.1
Once you get comfortable and "internalize" this tempo, try this same groove at faster and slower tempos
Method 2: Dotted-Half-Note Note Click Set your metronome to 40 BPM with no accented clicks. Treating each click with a value of adotted half note; play the above groove (Ex 9.1).
