Unit 8 - Chapter 2
Chapter 2: Hand-Foot Patterns The second part of this system is playing patterns between the hands and bass drum: In order to develop relaxed, smooth flow of notes using these three limbs, you must be very careful of two main aspects: 1) balance of sound between the hands and foot,and 2) precise note placement: The patterns are organized by note grouping in the following manner:
Two-note patterns one on the hands and one on the foot: Ex 8.13
Three-note patterns two on the hands and one on the foot; or one in the hands and two on the foot. Ex 8.14
Four-note patterns two on the hands and two on the foot Ex 8.15
Five-note patterns three on the hands and two on the foot; Ex 8.16 T 3
