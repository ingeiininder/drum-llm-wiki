About the Authors
Stewart Jean was born and raised in Deptford, New Jersey, where he was introduced to music through his father's love of classical music and his friends' Led Zeppelin LPs. Stewart's passion for music grew as he studied drumset; timpani and mallets in high school. Having attained many awards in state competitions, Stewart decided to set his goals on becoming a professional musician:

At age eighteen, Stewart enrolled in the Studio Music and Jazz Program at the University of Miami where he honed his performance and writing skills Upon graduating with a Bachelor of Music degree; Stewart became a sought-after freelance musician in the Miami area performing on many recordings, tours and shows.
After many successful years in Miami; Stewart relocated to Los Angeles; maintaining steady workflow: Stewart has performed or recorded behind many prominent artists such as Jimmy Buffett; Raul Midon, Preston Smith and Bruce Kulick Stewart's direct and to-the- point drumming has kept him at the top of many artists' lists.
Stewart is currently the Program Chair for the Drum Program at Musicians Institute in Hollywood; California: Having received expert mentoring throughout his years, Stewart has developed his educational offerings towards the goal of providing clear and positive nurturing to young students Stewart has created diverse curricula for the school such as Analysis of Great Grooves, Drum Set Soloing and Introduction to Keyboards.
