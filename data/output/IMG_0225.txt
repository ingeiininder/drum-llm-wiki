Meter Meter can be thought of as the pulse; the heartbeat of a piece of music: All music has momentum: a forward moving pulse that is counted in beats. Meter is measured in groups of beats with the most common being groups of two, three or four beats. For example:
Meter 2 Beats 3 Beats Beats
