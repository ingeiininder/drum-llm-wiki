Chapter 2: Hybrid Rudiments Hybrid rudiments (or "compound" rudiments) are defined as two or more rudiments combined to produce a new rudiment (i.e "flam" + "paradiddle" ="flam paradiddle" or"flamadiddle") This new breed of rudiments, developed in the drum corps scene; have seeped into modern drum set playing through use by drummers such as Vinnie Colaiuta, Tommy Igoe; Jason Sutter and Glen Sobel. Creatively speaking; these hybrids are challenging; but they - will also expand your ability to play expressively:
