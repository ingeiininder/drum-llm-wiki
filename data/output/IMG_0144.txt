Fermata A fermata (extended ending; hold or pause) is an element of musical notation indicating that the note should be sustained for longer than its note value would indicate: Exactly how much longer it is held is up to the discretion of the drummer: It is also up to the drummer to "cut-off" the band and end this extended hold: Ex 2.11
