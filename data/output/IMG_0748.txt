Chapter 3: Performance Notes and Sound This classic R&B sound utilizes the same balance of sound used in rock and pop, but none of the kick; snare and hi-hat voices should ever overpower the groove. In general, the sounds should be warm and blend together evenly Thus, the optimum sounds are as follows:
Snare sound: center snare Bass drum: off the head and open: Hi-hat: light and even
