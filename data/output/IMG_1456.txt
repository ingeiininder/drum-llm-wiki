Jeff Bowders began his journey of drumming in 1987. Since then; he has been blessed with the opportunity to perform and/or teach in over 50 countries Whether it is backing artist such as Paul Gilbert; Puddle of Mudd, Shakira and host of  others, or performing as a solo artist at drum festivals and camps, Jeff's passion for drums and drumming is undeniable: An in-demand educator at Musicians Institute in Los Angeles since 2003, Jeff's critically acclaimed MI Press Hal Leonard Publications include Essential Rock Drumming Concepts book/CD; Double Bass Drumming-The  Mirrored Groove System book/CD; Essential Drumset Fills-The Component Rhythm System book/CD and Double BassDrumming Workshop DVD.
