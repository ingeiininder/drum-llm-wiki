During the second track, the guitar solo will be "open" and will conclude on the guitarist's cue: You will then D.S. to letter A and play through letter B to the coda. Important: In order to avoid a mundane and "flat" solo, it is extremely important that you build in intensity with the guitarist during this section: Ex 2.9 E7 A7
