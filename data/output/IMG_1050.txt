Chapter 2: Reading in 7/8 When reading in 7/8,the eighth note gets the beat; therefore every eighth note is a downbeat: The number seven should be verbalized as "sev" to eliminate the second syllable: Ex 6.4
With eighth notes receiving every downbeat; I6th notes are now counted as 1-&-2-&-3-&-4-&-5-&-6-&-7-7 as follows: Ex 6.5 1E33=3== 1   & 2   & 3   & 4 & 5  & 6  & 7  &
Similar to counting in 5/8,it is recommended to initially count all beat numbers (1-2-3-4-5-6-7) when reading in 7/8. At faster tempos (and other varying situations) 7/8 can be broken up into smaller groups of two and three eighth notes in order to accommodate the rhythmic phrasing and patterns
