Chapter 3: The Drum Break One Eight-Bar Chorus of Drum Solo Brazilian drumming has a strong tradition of drum solos and breakdowns. Therefore; this week's lesson features an eight-bar drum break in which you will play a written drum solo over the bossa foot pattern Thus; you must maintain the bass drum and hi-hat patterns with your feet while playing the written drum solo:
This drum break will occur on the B section of the second chorus of the guitar solo After the drum break; you will DS. back to A and take the coda after the fourth bar of B.
