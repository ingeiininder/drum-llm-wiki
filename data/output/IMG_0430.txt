In Use Ties are normally utilized within the following scenarios:
Extending a Note Value across a Bar Line A tie enables a note to sustain past the allowable number of beats within one measure
Quarter Notes: Thus (and in the example below), the quarter note on beat 4 is tied toa whole note; which is nota new note to be played on the downbeat of 1. It is simply extending the value of the previous quarter note on beat 4 like this: Ex 8.3 2 3 2 3 4
Half note: worth two beats
Quarter note: worth one beat
Quarter note tied to whole note: worth five beats
Eighth Note: To illustrate this further; here we see an eighth note on the"&"of 4,which is also tied to whole note as follows: Ex 8.4
2
3   &  4   & 1 2 3 4 7
Half note: worth two beats
Three eighth notes: worth one and a half beats
Eighth note tied to a whole note: worth four and a half beats
