Begin with your heel up a few inches from the pedal plate with your toes/ball of foot resting towards the top ofthe pedal (Ex.4.18). Slightly raise your toes to separate the two hi-hat cymbals, then, using the weight ofyou leg; play down through your heel and toes to produce a solid "chick" sound.
Ex 4.18
To achieve a tight, articulate "chick" sound in both methods, the two hi-hat cymbals have to have enough space to generate the sound: A minimum of one inch between the cymbals in necessary to produce this sound (Ex. 4.19);anything less will be a compromise (Ex. 4.20).
