Chapter 2: French Grip
In French grip, the palms are facing directly towards each other in a vertical manner; the thumbs will be on top and aligned with the sticks (Ex. 2.5).
Ex 2.5
To position the sticks into French grip hold you hand out as ifyou were to shake another's hand (Ex. 2.6). Now place the stick in between the thumb and first crease of your forefinger and lightly wrap the rest of your fingers around the stick (Ex 2.7).
