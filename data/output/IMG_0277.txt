Chapter 2: D. C.al Fine D. Cal Fine Da Capo is a musical term in Italian, meaning "from the beginning:" It is often abbreviated DC. and it is a directive to return to the beginning of the chart and repeat the previous part of music often used to save space: "Fine" itself signifies "the end" Together; D.C al Fine instructs the player to return to the beginning of the chart and play until the fine (end) As displayed below, fine may occur at any point in the chart and not necessarily at the bottom: Ex 3.13
