Chapter 3: The 4/4 to Cut Time Modulation 4/4 to Cut Time: Overview In this example; the chart will go from I6th-note funk feel in 4/4 to a samba in cut time: The funk section is written in 4/4 in order to display 16th-note patterns, which are idiomatic to the style The samba section is written in cut time to notate the ensemble figures as simply as possible; using larger note values. Also, the samba is known as a two-bar pattern; writing it in 4/4 would mistakenly notate the samba as a one- bar pattern:
