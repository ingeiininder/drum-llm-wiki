*Anote on tension: When you are practicing the continuous I6th notes on the hi-hat, you must be aware of any tension in both your hand and arm. It is imperative that you stay loose and relaxed. You will know that you are tense if there is no space between your thumb and index finger: For example: Ex 3.10
Correct: No tension present
Incorrect: Tension present
Tension: Sound Tension is harder to recognize when you are young and still developing your drumming skills. The desire to learn something (without paying attention to the physicality behind it) can very detrimental to your progress in the long run Not only is it physically very dangerous to play with tension; but the sound produced also suffers To hear this for yourself; play a ride cymbal with the stick held tightly in your hand The sound will be very tight and pingy: Now play the ride with the stick loose in your hand (imagine you are holding an egg). Upon striking the cymbal, you will hear a bright; vibrant and wide sound. Please keep this in mind when practicing and performing:
