Musical Note: Setting up a New Feel and Subdivision Many (if not all) professional drummers properly set up the transition between swung and straight feels They accomplish this by playing a fill one bar before the new section, using the upcoming section's feel and subdivision This allows the drummer to properly"cue"the band that a new feel is about to occur: Ifyou fail to do this, the new section will appear"out of nowhere" and not sound musical: Here are a few setup ideas for the last bar of the head: Ex 7.8
