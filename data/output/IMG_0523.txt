Chapter 6: The Drum Seat One of the most overlooked aspects of playing the drums is the seat and where to sit on it. The reason why it may be difficult to play the drumset comfortably and consistently is because it requires the use of all four limbs. Generally we use our feet to balance ourselves; but while playing the kit we need to have as much freedom with our feet as we do with our hands This means we can no longer dependon our feet for_balance while_plaving drums: Our balance needs to center on our seat:
To create this shift of balance from our feet to our seat we need to sit towards the backside of the drum throne (Ex. 2.28). Sitting at the front of the throne will transfer all of your weight directly down to your feet thus shifting your center of gravity (balance) to your feet (Ex 2.29).
