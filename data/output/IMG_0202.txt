Multi-Rests Another way to indicate multiple measures of rests is to use multi-rest. A multi-rest is a thick; black horizontal bar with a number above it The black bar indicates a rest and the number indicates the number of measures In this chart; the four bars of rest for the drummer are notated by use of a multi-rest For example: Ex 9.3
Although this is a simple concept; it is easy to get lost in a multi-rest (especially in very long ones). One method to counting through a multi-rest is done by adding the measure number: For example: Ex 9.4
"Two" "Three
"2"
"Two" "Three"
"3"
"Two" "Three"
"4"
"Two" "Three"

In the A section the first and second endings are identical The first and second endings are notated by being housed under the same bracket: The third ending has a written fill. For example: Ex 9.5
