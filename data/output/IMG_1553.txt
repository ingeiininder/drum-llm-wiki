9. Stab
This is another one of my favorite tunes. There is great question-and-answer interplay between the bass guitar and horns in the A section. It is important not to play too busy at first, since you do not want to clutter the horn parts, but rather compliment them: Make fill choices that sit well with the horns and bass guitar line parts
