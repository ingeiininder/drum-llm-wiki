Riding on a Crash Cymbal During letter B, the riding surface is a crash cymbal: When riding on crash cymbal, you must use the shoulder of the stick just under the tip. To produce a washy sound with minimum subdivision (the desired effect), you must strike the edge of the cymbal at a 450angle: Your wrist should be relaxed and each stroke should be consistent; avoiding any accenting: Lastly, make sure that this washy cymbal sound does not overpower the snare and bass drum: Ex 1.12
In general,you must be sure to stay physically relaxed when playing aggressive grooves Ifyou get too tense you will choke the sound, causing your groove and sound production to suffer: Be sure to recognize tension in your body (especially your mouth; toes and hands) when practicing:
