Building the Letter A Pattern The hand/foot pattern between the snare drum/floor tom and bass drum must be executed with precision or it will sound lopsided. A great way to practice this pattern is to break down each grouping of four eighth notes. By isolating each component of this pattern you can focus on subdividing eighth notes and tighten up the space between the double stops and the bass drum: For example: Ex 1.3b
