The un-accented note: In order to achieve this sound, you will use the tip of the stick, just beneath the tip. Next; use this part of the stick to strike the body of the cymbal. However (because you are striking the bell on the downbeats)  you will not be able to strike the ride in the normal spot: Your hand will not be able to reposition itself that quickly: Therefore, you will use the tip of the stick on the left-hand center part of the cymbal: (to the left of the bell itself). For example: Ex 2.22
