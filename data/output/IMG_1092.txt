Unit 10 - Chapter 2
Chapter 2: Exercises There are two exercises in this chapter; the first being a snare drum etude and the second a drum set performance piece; both incorporating rhythmic elements from the courses Drum Reading 1-4.
Areas of focus: Rhythmic values: Whole; half; quarter; eighth; 16th, and 32nd notes and rests; half , quarter- , eighth-, and 16th-note triplets (and rests) Dotted and tied notes Time signatures (including odd meters) Meter and time signature changes Chart form (road map) Dynamics Lead sheet interpretation: ensemble figure orchestration and set-up fills, timekeeping figures and groove creation (drum set specific)
