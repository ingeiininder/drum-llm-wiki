Unit fOUN reading | techNIque pertOrMaNce Chapter 1: Velocity and Endurance Exercises Two important aspects of developing proper drumming technique are velocity and endurance: In drumming; velocity relates to the speed and direction an object is moving: Endurance is the ability to maintain velocity. These two areas are developed through muscle control and strengthening: Endurance is diminished when muscles tense up. Thus, if you want to play fast and loud, you must stay loose and relaxed. The following exercises are designed to help you develop both velocity and endurance while maintaining control.
