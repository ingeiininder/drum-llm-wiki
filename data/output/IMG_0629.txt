In Reading 1, Unit 6 you learned about swung eighth-note notation: In this unit you will learn about swung 16th-note notation. Swung I6th notes are widely used in modern rock; funk and hip-hop. To economize notation; the rhythms in these genres are normally written as regular I6th notes to avoid monotonous usage of rests and brackets: Swung I6th notes are based on 16th-note triplets. Similar to the "broken eighth-note triplet" needed to create a shuffle feel,a swung I6th-note feel employs a "broken I6th-note triplet" To create broken I6th-note triplets; we remove the first and fifth note.
