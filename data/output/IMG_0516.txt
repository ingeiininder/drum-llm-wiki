The German grip primarily uses the wrist, which makes it great for power and utilizing the Moeller technique that we will be addressing in the next unit: Although this technique doesn't favor the use of fingers, it does place them in an effective position to stop the rebound for our down strokes; which we explored in the previous unit:
When beginning to explore this technique make sure your elbows don't flare out too far like a chicken wing; keep them relatively close to your sides. You can check this by seeing ifyour sticks have formed an angle greater than 908 (Ex. 2.4).
