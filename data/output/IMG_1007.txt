Chapter 2: Mixed 32nd Note Etudes As stated earlier; it is very important for drummers to develop an advanced ability to interpret ensemble figures: Adding 32nd notes to your repertoire of ideas will greatly enhance your ability to set up figures and play fills in between figures. Reading 2 presented the concept of reading simple figures as accented notes within a steady stream of continuous, alternating I6th notes.
