Half-Time Shuffle and Half-Time Shuffle Feel In today's lesson you are going to play a half-time shuffle feel. Please do not confuse this with a standard half-time shuffle: In a standard half-time shuffle groove; the backbeat is placed on beat 3 and performed as eighth- note triplets. For example:
Standard Half-Time Shuffle Groove (Backbeat on 3) Ex 4.1
However; in todays chart you are going to play a half-time shuffle feel This groove is phrased as 16th-note triplets and it places the backbeat on beats 2 and 4. For example:
Half-Time Shuffle Feel (Backbeat on 2 and 4) Ex 4.2
