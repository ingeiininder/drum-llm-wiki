Chapter 2: Moving around the Drum Set Now that you are comfortable alternating between grooving and reading rhythms, let's begin moving your hands around the drum set Instead of reading the rhythms only on the snare drum; we are now going to incorporate the tom-toms It is very important to become comfortable adding motion around the set while reading rhythms. This will help develop your creativity and will make you play very musically: You can add melody to your fills by descending down the toms or ascending up the toms Creating melodic phrases by moving around the drum set will add a new dimension to your playing:
