About the Authors
A native of Denver; Colorado, Ryan Brown has   played drums and percussion since he was 11 years old, when he made the fateful switch from trumpet in elementary school band. He spent countless hours in his parents'basement over the next seven years honing his craft: He then headed to Indiana University; where he studied with rock legend Kenny Aronoff and graduated with Bachelor of Music degree in jazz studies and percussion, earning the university's prestigious Performer's Certificate as well;
During and after college; Ryan did tours playing on the S/S Norway for Norwegian Cruise Line; sharpening his jazz performance and reading skills and seeing the world He moved to Los Angeles in 1999,and worked his way up in the music scene; becoming one of the busiest live and session drummers in town:
In addition to his gig as the drummer for Zappa Plays Zappa, Ryan plays with Los Angeles-based bands Black Belt Karate and Madras, and in the bands of artists Arrica Rose, Keram Malicki-Sanchez and Space Oddity, among others. He has performed with many prominent artists, including Taiwanese pop star Chyi- Chin; Ingrid Michaelson; Niki Haris, Frank Stallone and the Norwegian Wind Ensemble:
Ryan played drums on thehit single "The Real Thing"from Bo Bice, and can be heard on releases by Foreigner Circus Diablo, Hannah Montana, Clay Aiken, Cassie Davis, Madame Mayhem, Nick Lachey, Jesse McCartney, Heather Graham, Suzie McNeil (Rock Star INXS) and Stephanie McIntosh; as well as on the movie soundtracks for Kung Fu Panda (Hans Zimmer and John Powell), The Dark Knight (Hans Zimmer) and Ice Age: Continental Drift (Adam Schlesinger) . He's performed on recordings with Alex Lifeson, Earl Slick; Elliot Easton and Rudy Sarzo:
He is member of the drum faculty at Musicians Institute, and helped write the school's new drum curriculum as well:
