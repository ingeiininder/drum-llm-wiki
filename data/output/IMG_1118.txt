Riding the Floor Tom on Eighth Notes This eighth-note pattern should be played evenly and medium- forcefully at a dynamic of mezzo-forte: In order to achieve this sound, you will use the tip of the stick, with a loose right-hand grip; to strike the center of the floor tom. This will give you a thick and resonant floor tom tone Important: Please be careful to avoid playing just above or below the center of the head. If this occurs, you will not hear the power and fundamental tone of the floor tom:
Musical note: This technique should make the floor tom sound Iike a"low drone"that surrounds the entire band. Ifyou hit the floor tom too hard, off-center or with a tight/tense grip, it will sound too staccato, choked and stiff:
