Chapter 5: Seven-Stroke Ruff Standard Seven-Stroke Ruff This rudiment consists of seven alternating strokes; the first six are unaccented and the seventh stroke is accented The note values used to practice this rudiment will be I6th-note triplets and quarter notes. Ex 4.13
