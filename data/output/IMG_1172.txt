Snare Drum The snare drum should be played very consistently and at a dynamic of forte (loud). Additionally; each snare hit should be a rim shot: This stroke is executed by hitting the center of the drumhead and rim at the same exact time. A rim shot is loudest (and most effective) when the stroke is started by the wrist and finished off by the fingers Let's take a look at both steps:
