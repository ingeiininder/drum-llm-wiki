UnIt Ne reading | techNIque perfOrMance Chapter 1: Displaced Grooves The ability to play a specific sequence of notes and shift the pattern by one eighth note or 16th note before or after the beat will offer exciting possibilities in your rhythmic vocabulary. Initially, these ideas will challenge your mind or"ear" more than the physical component Becoming comfortable hearing certain phrases that begin and end in unfamiliar places within the time will unlock certain rhythmic plateaus and give you a fresh understanding of what is possible with beat placement and its affects on music By displacing beats, we can create the illusion that the time has temporarily shifted to an "odd-meter" However; by knowing where you are within the time and being able to resolve the displaced beat will guarantee the meter has not changed; this why counting is essential when practicing and performing this concept: KNOW WHERE YOU AREI
