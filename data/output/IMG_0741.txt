Unit 4 - Chapter 2
Bass Drum Sound The bass drum should be big and warm, So maintain the "off-the-head" sound, but play with force and conviction: Burying the beater will cause the drum to choke up and not produce a "round" sound.
Ride Cymbal The ride cymbal should be played with the tip of the stick; but a few"shouldered" strikes will help to spread the sound: By occasionally shouldering the ride; a large amount sonic space gets filled which allows for a more relaxed feel. For the last note (fermata) you should play a very quiet double-stroke roll a few inches from the bell of the ride or crash cymbal: Ex 4.15
