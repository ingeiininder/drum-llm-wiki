Chapter 3: Metric Modulation Similar to implied metric modulation_but very different in terms of rhythmic composition_metric modulation establishes a set pattern in one note value and then applies that same pattern to a different note value: This is a very effective technique in creating an illusion of time speeding up or slowing down. Just as we have learned with the previous two concepts, counting is imperative here.
