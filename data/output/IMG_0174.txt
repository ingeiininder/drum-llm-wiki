Letter B: Fills and Figures Within the first ending of letter B there is a direction to play a two-beat fill. Remember that this fill is only leading the song back to a repeat of B,so it would be appropriate to keep this fiIll on the subtle side: The second ending of letter B containsan ensemble figure of a half note. This figure should be played on a crash cymbal with a bass drum supporting it. The notated snare drum hit on beat 3 should be played as a rimshot with one stick while your free hand should grab the crash cymbal on beat 3. The technique will allow for an incredibly dramatic stop and dead silence on beat 4 For example: Ex 5.15
