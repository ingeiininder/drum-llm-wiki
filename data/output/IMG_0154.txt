D.C. al Fine: D.C (da capo) is a term that tells you to return to the beginning of the chart. Al Fine tells us stop playing once you reach the term Fine. Together; they tell us to return to the beginning and play through to the ending (which is marked by the word Fine). Let's take a look at how to properly navigate this structure:
Upon reaching bar 8 of the last A section (AABA) you will see the text: DC al Fine: You will immediately return to the beginning of the chart (as indicated by the solid arrow). You will now play through the entire AABA form again (symbolized by the broken arrows). As you reach bar 8 of the last A, you will play through that measure and continue onward to measure 26.
