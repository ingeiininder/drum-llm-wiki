Repeat Instructions: 3X ("Three Times") At the end of letter B (the first time) you will see a repeat sign instructing you to return to letter A. Since the A and B sections need to be played three times, the instruction "3X"is placed by the repeat sign at the end of letter B. This does not mean to repeat three times but rather to play this sequence (letters A and B) three times in total. Ex 8.9 3X
