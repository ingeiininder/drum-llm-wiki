Solo No. 2 isa 17-bar solo (with first and second endings) in the time signature of 2/4.This solo incorporates many rudiments such as flams and drags (which we've already covered) as well as some ruffs and roll variations The rudimental breakdown is as follows: Bar I: 3-Stroke Ruff (playing into beat followed by 7-Stroke Ruff) Bar 2: 3-Stroke Ruffs Bars 4 & 5: Flam Taps (as eighth and 16th notes) Bar 6: Alternating Flams (as eighth notes) Bar 7: Flam Accents (as I6th/eighth notes) Bar 8: 7-Stroke Roll (starting on the"&"of beat 2) Bar 11: Single Flam Paradiddles Bar 13 & 14: Drag Paradiddle #2 Bar 16 & 17: 9-Stroke Rolls
